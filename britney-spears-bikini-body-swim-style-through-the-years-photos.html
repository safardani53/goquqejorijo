Britney Spears’ Sizzling Swim Style Through the Years: From Sexy One-Pieces to Teeny Bikinis

Sexy swim style! Britney Spears has a pretty amazing bikini body — and she doesn’t shy away from showing off her figure. The 40-year-old singer takes to the ‘gram time and again to show off her progress at the gym — and her super cute swimsuits.

Whether she’s in an itsy-bitsy cropped costume for a music video or sweating it out in a sports bra and shorts, the “Baby One More Time” singer looks great in just about everything. After all, she makes sure to stick to a solid gym routine. 

“Well … for the love of God I finally see some definition in my abs !!! It’s nice to finally see some results,” she wrote in an October 2021 Instagram.

So how does she stay so fit? She switches things up pretty frequently, but she does her fair share of weight lifting with fiancé Sam Asghari and dances on the regular (just take a peep at her Instagram for proof!). 

[ami-related id="1485654" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/celebrity-news/pictures/britney-spears-ups-and-downs-over-the-years/" title="Britney Spears’ Ups and Downs Over the Years" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/04/1998-84397994.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="1485651" format="gallery" flag="" channel="1"]

While Spears’ killer body remains a constant, her actual swim style has changed a decent amount over the years. Back in the early 2000’s, the superstar would opt for bolder looks, wearing polka dot prints and bandeau tops. 

But as she has gotten older, she's started to keep it a tad bit more understated, donning solid hues and traditional bikini cuts. That’s not to say that what she wears poolside is boring by any means. Even though it may be on the simple side, little ruffled details and pretty colors keep her two-piece game strong.

Plus, her favorite looks don’t break the bank! In fact, Target is one of her go-to places to scoop up new swimsuits. In August 2021, the “Circus” singer took to Instagram to show off her new striped blue and white suit from the retailer.

“My favorite new baby blue bathing suit I wore in Maui is from @Target 🎯… girls, you gotta go there cause their suits are bomb💣👙!!!! Psss I’m going to be 40 this year and as Debbie say in This Is 40 … I’m not ready to shop at old lady stores😂😂😂!!!” Spears captioned a photo.
