The Bachelor Finale: Sean Lowe and Catherine Giudici Ride Elephants in Thailand

The couple that rides elephants together…stays together? The Bachelor star Sean Lowe and finalist Catherine Giudici always have a good time together, and on the season finale, the pair take their romance to new heights — by riding an elephant in Thaliand.

“Oh my gosh, we’re riding an elephant!” Giudici, 26, exclaims in a preview clip of the March 11 finale. “What?!”

The free-spirited bachelorette has previously admitted to having trouble opening up emotionally, but Giudici says she felt completely at ease during their bumpy ride aboard the elephant — because she had Sean by her side.

“I feel like, unstable, but in control at the same time because I know Sean’s there,” she says. “We’re experiencing this together and it is just so cool.”

The Seattle-based graphic designer and the “hunky” bachelor (Giudici has called Lowe her “hunk” frequently throughout season) have had instant chemistry from the start, and the 29-year-old entrepreneur hasn’t been shy about his feelings toward her.

“I love your weirdness,” he told her during their final date aboard a boat in Thailand a few weeks back.

“It’s crazy. We’re like, in a relationship,” she said later that night with a giggle.
