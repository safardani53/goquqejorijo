TOWIE: What happened between Chloe and Dan? Wed 7 October 2020 21:00 Updated Mon 4 September

TOWIE’s Chloe Sims has revealed that her ex Dan Edgar was a rebound from Pete Wicks.

Since Chloe and Pete’s two-year “friends with benefits” romance was spilled, cast members of the ITV show have began to wonder if it happened while she was with Dan. However the preview shows Amber Turner saying that Dan was never officially with Chloe, leaving fans confused about their romance.

So what happened between Dan and Chloe? We done some research in a bid to found out what happened!

Chloe Sims and Dan Edgar attending the ITV Gala afterparty, November 9, 2017.

Chloe and Dan from TOWIE: Relationship timeline

The pair were initially close friends before they turned it into a romance. Dan was formerly in a relationship with Amber Turner – who he is now back with – until they split up just before Christmas in 2018.

It came after rumours that Dan’s heart was no longer with their relationship. He had denied these allegations but then later started dating Chloe in March 2019.

Dan and Chloe began dating in Thailand during filming for The Only Way is Essex. However Chloe’s friends weren’t happy after the revelation that Dan has already cheated.

What happened between Chloe and Dan?

They allegedly split after Chloe’s cousin Joey Essex and their mutual friends weren’t happy about the pair dating. Dan then reportedly broke up with Chloe after weeks of dating her.

It came after reports which suggested that Dan cheated on the TOWIE star, however he denied these claims.

Chloe told The Sun Online that he lied to her and destroyed her. She said:

He is not a bad person but the way he has gone about it and let the public think of me, I feel like he destroyed nine years of me building myself up to be transparent on TV. I feel like he might as well have got a bulldozer.

She added: “The abuse I got every single day was ‘desperate, plastic s**g. Ugly’ – it was so bad.”

Amber then returned to the series to reveal that she had slept with Dan before he had made a move on Chloe. In May 2019, Amber and Dan got back together and have been in a relationship ever since.

Chloe admitted to The Sun Online that it was the fact that she and Dan had been such close friends for so many years that had hurt her the most. They had been friends for six years.

Chloe reveals Dan was a ‘rebound’

The TOWIE star has admitted to co-star Georgia Kousoulou that Dan was a rebound and that she was with other men in a “revengeful” way against Pete Wicks. She said: “It wasn’t about Dan. It was about Pete.”

Chloe continued: “Love just sends you completely off your head.”

Pete also admits to the lads that Chloe saw Dan as a “bit of a rebound”, however it is not known if or how their “friends with benefits” romance overlapped with her and Dan’s.
