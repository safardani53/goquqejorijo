Pregnant Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry SLAMS fans who attacked her nude photoshoot with horse The

PREGNANT Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry has slammed fans who "attacked" her naked photo with a horse.

Kailyn, 27, faced a backlash after sharing a fully naked photo on Instagram while in Iceland as she celebrated her fourth pregnancy.

Not only did it reignite the feud with her long term enemy Briana DeJesus, hordes of trolls insulted her over her looks.

The mum-of-three has hit back at the nasty critics - whilst she hinted she's feeling "pain".

She wrote: "Trolls don't even know they're getting hits of dopamine when they attack other people online."

Then she added a quote: "'They feel powerful when they make someone else feel pain".

Loyal fans jumped to defend her, with one person saying: "I think ppl just didn't understand the horse. Kail shut these haters down... take another boudoir shoot in another location!

"You're beautiful and deserve to appreciate your pregnant body. Don't focus on them just create a better memory and shoot".

Another added: "I'm sorry you're constantly being body shame. You're beautiful inside and out. Their nasty opinions are not valid and I wish people would realize that before they start saying them."

A third remarked: "I wish trolls would see the heart [sic] and devastation it causes to people. So many people take their lives because of nasty horrible things said or written about them if you dont have anything nice to say stay quiet."

It comes after Briana DeJesus reignited their long-standing feud by laughing at the photo.

Someone shared a screenshot of the pic, writing, "No mam". Brianna then commented underneath the post with a series of crying laughing faces.

Kailyn bit back, writing: "Posting this photo didn't go as planned. #yikes but remember the time shock posted the video of y'all having sex [crying laughing emoji]."

This is in reference to Briana DeJesus' ex boyfriend Shakiel 'Shoc' Brown being arrested and charged with video voyeurism after he put videos of intimate moments between them on social media following their break up.

Deciding to get even more personal, Briana replied: "Girl shut the f**k up! Lol go continue getting beat up by Chris lol"

Kailyn said in a podcast things got "physical" with her on-again-off-again boyfriend and baby daddy Chris in their "toxic" relationship, but denies domestic abuse.

Briana then finished off the feud by backtracking on her comments: "I thought the photo was cute. The horse made the picture. I was laughing at my friends who made a comment. Revenge porn is nothing to talk about but since we bringing it up, guess I can being [sic.] stuff up tooo"

When a fan called her out for bringing up domestic abuse, she replied: "[The revenge porn incident] was the most depressing times I ever had in my whole entire life. U don't know if I was suicidal or not so shut the f*** up also".

Briana and Kailyn had a bit of an intense rivalry on-screen, but this boiled over when Briana dated Kail's ex-husband Javi Marroquin.

The two then got into a major physical fight during the August 6 2018 reunion special.
