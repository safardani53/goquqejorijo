TURNING 30 for Love Island's Anna Vakili meant that she should have not one but TWO parties to celebrate her special day.

Earlier on in the week the Love Island star had a small get together with her close family - but things certainly turned up a notch or two last night.

The reality star, who rose to fame on the 5th series of the popular ITV2 dating show, celebrated in style for her 30th birthday on Saturday evening.

Anna shared what she got up to with her 1.2m followers on Instagram throughout the night and it looked pretty amazing.

The beauty pulled out all the stops for the bash as she rented out a huge house to party the night away with her pals - including fellow Islanders Amber Gill, Joe Garratt and Anton Danyluk.

Her guests were welcomed at the door with a glass of bubbly held by a shirtless butler.

Moving inside the impressive grand hall way boasted a gorgeous chandelier, spiral staircase and a display of balloons to mark her special day.

The main room had a range of tasty treats for her guests to tuck into - including their very own bags of popcorn, an array of sweets and their very own cocktails in a jar.

Her pals certainly weren't short for food as another table boasted a range of delicious burgers, nachos and tacos.

Her pop-art themed cake was an impressive 3-tiers high and covered in mini macarones, strawberries and £50 notes.

There was also a Love Island themed table covered in special coasters and her guests had their very own personalised champagne flutes.

Anna also had some quirky treats that were inspired by her profession.

Her huge birthday blow out comes just days after she celebrated with a two-tiered cake, plush presents and lots of food.

The pharmacist turned reality star spent the whole of Tuesday with her parents and younger sister Mandi as they saw in the landmark day.

Anna posted the sentimental photos on social media surrounded by her family and designer gifts from Christian Dior and Fendi.

The two-tiered cake was covered in pink flowers with pink and gold frosting with white roses and strawberries.

The brunette beauty was also treated to a buffet of 'the best' Iranian food made by her mother.

The 30-year-old was dressed to impress in a low cut floral dress which showed off all her curves.

The stunning brunette wrote on social media: "30 TODAY!! I have been spending the day with my family eating S**T LOADS of Iranian food. It’s been perfect!!

"I’ve had so many lovely messages from friends and all of you! Which I am SOOOO grateful for.

"Thank you everyone for making me feel so loved and so special. I feel so blessed and lucky.

"My parents are the most amazing parents coming from Iran starting with nothing, they have given me the best 30 years and I love them so much. Let’s not forget my crazy little sister."

She continued: "Bring on the weekend for more celebrations p.s. My mum is the best Iranian cook ever and I’m not just saying that."

Anna was famed in the villa for her curves and plump pout - and has been described as "the British Kim Kardashian".
