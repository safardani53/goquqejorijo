Monica Garcia is suing Heather Gay on RHOSLC months after mystery phone call Thu 21 December 20

Monica Garcia is suing Heather Gay on RHOSLC in a lawsuit. It comes months after Heather Gay’s phone call from a mystery lady which fans reckon was a warning about Monica’s lawsuit.

The most popular theory is Heather Gay’s phone call is from someone who informed her Monica, a new cast member on The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City, intended to sue Heather’s business Beauty Lab + Laser. Now, the lawsuit launched by Monica is all unravelling on the Bravo series.

Monica suing Heather on RHOSLC

Beauty Lab + Laser filed a lawsuit against Monica in August 2023 for breach of contract and breach of the covenant of good faith and fair dealing, as per The Sun. The lawsuit claims Monica didn’t pay them.

The firm alleges Monica signed a contract with them on December 10, 2019, to pay $2,449 for cosmetic injection services. Documents state Monica only paid $449 of the initial payment.

She was supposed to make $200 monthly payments for the next ten months however never did, the documents claim. Beauty Lab + Laser argue it “suffered and continues to suffer significant losses”.

Inside Monica Garcia’s lawsuit

As a result of Monica not paying the full amount, business requested the court to determine at trial how much – ”not less than $2,000″ – Monica should hand over, as well as “reasonable attorney’s fees”.

In September 2023, Monica filed a counterclaim against Beauty Lab + Laser for breach of independent duty of care, breach of contract, and breach of good faith and fair dealing.

In the documents obtained by The Sun, Monica confirmed she only paid Beauty Lab + Laser $449 for her total payment because she believed she “received defectively, negligently given injections”.

She claimed the injections “did not have the intended, promised result.” As a result of the “botched” injections, Monica claimed she had to go to another beautician to fix her nose and lips.

Heather Gay received mystery phone call

Heather had a mystery phone call during RHOSLC’s season premiere, but fans won’t see the conversation until January. While in Bermuda on May 10, 2023, she picks up the phone. “Hey, what did you find out?” Heather tells the caller.

“Are you kidding me right now? Shut the f*** up,” Heather continued to tell cameras, “I’m trembling. I cannot believe it’s her. How could she do this to us? I’m freaking the f*** out.”

She then added: “How did we all fall for it? I don’t know who to trust anymore.” Heather didn’t want crews around and forces the camera person toward the exit and told them: “You guys, seriously, no.”
