Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry wishes son Lux a happy 4th birthday as she &amp; sons recover from Covid after Dom

TEEN Mom Kailyn Lowry wished her son Lux a very happy 4th birthday as she and her sons recover from Covid after their Dominican Republic trip.

Kailyn, 29, shared throwback snapshots of her little one on Instagram. 

In the adorable snaps, Lux appeared with his stepbrothers Isaac, 11, and Lincoln, 7.

The kids appeared to be having fun together in the snaps. 

The Teen Mom star shares Isaac with Jo Rivera and Lincoln with Javi Marroquin. 

Kailyn captioned the photos: "I cannot believe it’s been 4 years already."

'SICK IS NEVER FUN'

Last month, the MTV star had to cancel the combined birthday party of Creed, one, and Lux - both of whom she shares with her ex-baby daddy Chris Lopez.

The mom-of-four then took to her Instagram Stories to explain that she hoped they could still go ahead with party plans while it was summer.

Kailyn told her followers: "I'm supposed to be getting ready for a birthday party for Creed and for Lux, but unfortunately it had to be canceled because we're so sick and the headache I have right now is off the charts.

"Taking care of a baby when you're sick is never fun."

She added: "We're going to reschedule the birthday party and hopefully everyone can make it, since we're nearing the end of summer and everyone has 100 things going on."

'QUARANTINED IMMEDIATELY'

The Teen Mom 2 star revealed that she caught the COVID virus during her vacation to the Dominican Republic.

Recently, the 29-year-old host slammed her haters during her recent Coffee Convos podcast.

Many trolls claimed that she was a Covid-19 "superspreader."

After talking about her "congestion" from the sickness, the TV personality explained to Lindsie Chrisley how she and her family all caught it equally.

She said: "People are online talking s**t saying that I'm a super spreader and things like that but my entire family came home with, I didn't give my whole family Corona.

"We were all in the same places at the exact same time. I told the story on Baby Mamas but I am not a super spreader."
