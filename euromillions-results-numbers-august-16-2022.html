EuroMillions results and numbers: National Lottery draw tonight, August 16

THE draw for tonight's National Lottery EuroMillions (August 16, 2022) has taken place, with life-changing cash prizes at stake.

You can find out if you’re a winner by checking your ticket against tonight's numbers below.
