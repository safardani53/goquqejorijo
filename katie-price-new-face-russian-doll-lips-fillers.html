Katie Price reveals her new face with Russian doll lips that touch her nose after getting fill

KATIE Price revealed her new face with ‘Russian doll lips’ that touch her nose after getting new fillers.

The former glamour model, 45, headed to Lift Aesthetics clinic, which has clinics all over the world including London, Essex, Dubai and Ireland.

Katie, who is no stranger to plastic surgery, videoed herself shortly after getting them done and proudly showed off her new plumped pout.

The former Page 3 favourite said in the clip: "Here we go! I've got my new Russian doll lips done from when Ellie dissolved them...

"I'm having to keep topping them up obviously because where she dissolved them, and I love it!"

The star then blew a kiss to the camera and said that they look swollen straight after the procedure.

Katie added: "They can even touch my nose! Love it!"

Back in September Katie told fans that she was having more surgery in a bid to "start fresh".

The mum-of-five shared with her followers that she was going to dissolve her lip fillers and then make more tweaks on her face.

Weeks later she showed off her 'real' lips as she got her fillers dissolved in preparation for her Russian doll lip fillers.
