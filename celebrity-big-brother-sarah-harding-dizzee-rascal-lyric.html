Celebrity Big Brother's Sarah Harding sends fans into meltdown as they spot she used a Dizzee Rascal

SARAH Harding outed herself as a secret Dizzee Rascal fan after quoting the rapper during a blazing row with Chad Johnson.

The Girls Aloud star, 35, told her American boyfriend to "fix up, look f***ing sharp" while accusing him of not taking her advice or listening to her on Monday night's episode.

Viewers immediately recognised Sarah had been inspired by Dizzee's 2003 track, 'Fix Up Look Sharp' and took to social media in droves to share their amusement at her choice of words during the fight.

One commented: "You can't have an argument with your boyfriend and throw in a Dizzee Rascal line."

Another said: "Watched a clip of that Sarah Harding arguing with some fella in and she f***ing quoted Dizzee Rascal but didn't acknowledge it."

A third added: "Cannot watch Sarah anymore without cringing. Now quoting Dizzee Rascal! WTF!"

A fourth wasn't impressed either, tweeting: "Sarah spitting those Dizzee Rascal lyrics. Embarrassed."

And a fifth chimed in: "Did Sarah Harding just quote Dizzee Rascal when trying to have a serious argument?!"

During the explosive row on Sunday's episode, an agitated Sarah told Chad: "I've been in this business for 15 f***ing years and you're just starting out, mate. Don't f***ing take the p**s.

"I won't stand for it. Fix up, look f***ing sharp and listen to someone who has had the experience, been there, done that, worn the T-shirt and been persecuted most of her career for it.

"You're not listening to my advice and you get defensive and it's out of order.

"I'm trying to look out for you so don't turn around and shove it in my face like a pile of s***.

"Don't f***ing cross me."

Chad appeared baffled about what he'd done wrong and decided to walk away from the argument, telling her: "Do you think I care?

"I spent 30 years of my life not giving a f*** about anything because my entire life sucked.

"You can't make my life any worse.

"I'm miserable every f***ing day of my life."

However, he soon came back with a smile on his face when Sarah called him.

"I'm very upset because I'm upset and shouldn't be smiling!" he told her.

"This is the weirdest fight ever! Can we make out a little bit?"

Clearly there were no hard feelings on Chad's side, as the couple then climbed into bed together where Chad confessed he LOVED the pop star after just three weeks of knowing her.

But one person unconvinced by the pair's romance is former housemate Jordan Davies, who claimed the pair are "using" each other for publicity.

In a chat with Star magazine, he alleged: "It's all a show. Chad is using Sarah to get as far as he can and Sarah's doing the same - it's all about storylines, it's all bulls***.

"Chad's an amazing man and Sarah's holding him back.

"We don't get on because she always blames me.

"She accused me of not looking up to her elders, but I always respect my elders. That's why I was like, 'Come on!'"

Jordan was evicted from the Celebrity Big Brother house last week - and memorably shocked host Emma Willis by declaring in his exit interview that he "hates" Sarah.

He might be coming face to face with her sooner rather than later, however, as Sarah faces eviction tonight.

The singer is one of six celebrities on the chopping block during Tuesday evening's double eviction.

She faces off against Chad, Sam Thompson, Sandi Bogle, Jemma Lucy and Helen Lederer.
