Celebrities Who Regret Their Tattoos [PHOTOS]

Tattoos can be a permanent reminder of the person you love. They can also be a permanent reminder of a crazy night out you can't remember, an ex you'd rather forget, or even just the power of careful copy-editing.

These 24 celebrities have opened up about having to rethink their ink for a variety of reasons. Whether they no longer relate to an image they loved when they were young, split from someone who inspired a name tattoo somewhere intimate or realized (too late!) that there was a typo, see why celebrities including Megan Fox, Olivia Wilde and Blake Shelton have had tattoo regrets.

Megan Fox

The Tattoo: The word "Brian" written in elaborate script on her pelvis in tribute to ex-husband Brian Austin Green, two whom she was married from 2010 to 2021.

The Status: Inked over with a serpentine and floral design that incorporates cosmic elements. "Was able to completely hide the old tattoo underneath the new snake and flowers she wanted," wrote artist Jesse Krydick on the reveal photo. "Thanks for your trust @meganfox."

Olivia Wilde

The Tattoo: A "really bad one" she got when she was 13 of a dragon on her right butt cheek (which she called an "ass stamp" when recounting the story to Craig Ferguson). "I was in New York City and I thought it was a great idea, it had a lot of meaning at the time but now it's hideous."

The Status: Still there and worth celebrating! In March 2023, the actress gave her followers a good look at the ink in a 39th birthday post, which she captioned, "39 and feeling fine ... Here's to whatever's next. Probably not another tramp stamp but who knows."

Kal Penn

The Tattoo: The actor wanted to get a meaningful tattoo when he turned 40, so after having his genealogy traced on the show Finding Your Roots, he chose to ink R1A-M147, representing his maternal haplogroup (which links your DNA to millions of others going back to the same common ancestor) on his arm.

But after doing his own DNA test for fun, he pulled up the results on the screen and realized he was off by a number: It was really R1A-M417.

The Status: After enduring some ribbing from his partner Josh, Penn decided to keep it. "It's a good story," he told Kelly Clarkson on her talk show.

Hailey Bieber

The Tattoo: One of the model's 20+ tattoos is a handgun on her middle finger she got when she was 18. "I was like, 'Yeah! That looks cool,' " she told Elle. "But now, as a 24-year-old, I would never do that. I think guns are violent."

The Status: Still there. "It is what it is," she wrote on Instagram in 2015.
