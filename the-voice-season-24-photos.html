Photos of the Coaches, Finalists and More Hollywood Life

It’s all led to this. ‘The Voice’ has 5 finalists competing to be the season 24 champion. See photos of the finalists as the second night of the finale gets underway.

The Voice season 24 premiered on September 24 with Niall Horan, Reba McEntire, John Legend, and Gwen Stefani as coaches. This marked the first season without Blake Shelton, who left the show after 23 seasons.

Over the course of season 24, The Voice has witnessed some of the most incredible talent. After weeks of competition, the top 5 for season 24 are Huntley, Jacquie Roar, Mara Justine, Ruby Leigh, and Lila Forde. The contestants took the stage during night one of the finale in hopes of winning America’s vote. They’ll perform again on December 19 with their coaches and will be joined by several special performers. Scroll down to see the best photos of The Voice season 24.
