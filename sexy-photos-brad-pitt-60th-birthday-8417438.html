Sexy Photos of Brad Pitt for His 60th Birthday

Like his character in the 2008 film The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, Brad Pitt seems to be aging in reverse.

Celebrating his 60th birthday on Dec. 18, 2023, it seems as if the actor still looks like he did at 30, with his movie-star smile, rugged features and enviable hair.

In a 2019 interview with GQ, he spoke about getting older, and how it has changed the way he approaches both life and work.

"I hit this point in the late '90s or early 2000s, where I realized I was chasing these interesting [roles], yet I was failing to live as interesting a life as I thought I could," he said. “Maybe it's just getting older too, man.”
