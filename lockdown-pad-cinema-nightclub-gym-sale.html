Ultimate lockdown pad with cinema, nightclub and gym goes on sale for 9.5m

BRITAIN'S ultimate lockdown home has gone on the market - and the property comes with its own cinema, gym and even a nightclub.

The stunning house in Essex's millionaires' row boasts plenty of luxury additions perfect for anyone stuck indoors - as long as they've got a spare £9.5million.

The four-storey detached property - listed on Rightmove - in Benfleet boasts five bedrooms, all of which have en-suite bathrooms, while the master bedroom at the rear of the house comes with 'his-and-hers' dressing rooms. 

The new owner will walk into the home through a spectacular triple-height entrance hall which gives access to the main spaces, including two formal sitting rooms and a dining room.

But buyers are likely to be attracted to some of the property's unusual additions - and there's something for every taste.

Vino lovers and academics will enjoy the huge building's custom-designed wine cellar, study and library.

Home cooks will find a huge fitted kitchen and family room, which features a lift to utility rooms in the basement.

Kids - and big kids - will enjoy a series of rooms dedicated to entertainment, including a casino with life-sized Batman and Superman statues and a fully-equipped games room.

There's even a 5,500 sq ft nightclub with bar, dance floor, stage, lounge and dining spaces - and a bespoke 18-seat cinema.

For fitness lovers, the huge property features a large gym with sauna and solarium, as well as an indoor exercise pool and an additional pool house with a leisure pool, 10-seater spa, sauna and extensive changing facilities. 

And for those who prefer to exercise outdoors, there are three acres of land around the property, complete with a floodlit tennis court and five-a-side football pitch. 

The lucky new owner will also come home to a garage with space for four cars.
