A FORMER McDonald’s employee has revealed what it was really like to work at the fast-food chain.

Ash took to social media to talk about the types of customers the restaurants regularly have to deal with. 

The former employee, who goes by the name of ashleighswurld on TikTok, revealed four different types of people in an acted out video for her followers. 

In one example, she acts out a conversation between her and a customer at McDonald's.

She first starts off by saying: “Hi, what can I get for you?”

Ash then acts out the customer's response by saying: “Hi, would I be able to just get a latte?”
