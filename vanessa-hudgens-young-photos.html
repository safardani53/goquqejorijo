Photos From Her Young Days to Today Hollywood Life

Vanessa Hudgens has been in the spotlight forever. Celebrate her 35th birthday with her best photos over the years.

Vanessa Hudgens has been a star since her start. The High School Musical diva, born Dec. 14, 1988, made her screen debut in 2003’s Thirteen, before getting her big break on the Disney Channel. She starred in three HSM films, before moving onto more mature roles like Spring Breakers (2012), Second Act (2018), and Bad Boys for Life (2020).

More recently, she’s starred in The Princess Switch movies and the movie-musical Tick, Tick… Boom! She’s also hosted a number of high-profile affairs, including MTV’s 2022 Movie & TV Awards.

Through it all, Vanessa’s been a total sweetheart. Ready to see more photos of Vanessa through the years? Check out her gallery for all the best photos, from her teen days to today.
