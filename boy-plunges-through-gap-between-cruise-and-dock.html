AN ALARMING video shows the moment a panicked woman screams as she tries to find an eight-year-old boy who plunged into the sea.

The kid has fallen down a crack between a cruise ship and the dock and the hysterical woman can be heard shouting "where is he?"

The blood-curdling sounds are believed to be from the boy's mother but a child's screams can also be heard in the video.

The child, thought to be from northern Europe, was with his parents and sister on holiday in Salerno, Italy, when the nightmare occurred.

He was holding his father's hand shortly before he broke away and began to run along the harbour wall.

The youngster then slipped and fell into a tight gap between a cruise ship and the dock, according to local media.

The boy's desperate dad immediately followed him into the water in an attempt to rescue his son, but this lead to both becoming trapped.

Screams ring out from those around and passersby were quick to react.

They can be seen scrambling to pull the pair to safety before it's too late.

The grateful woman can be seen racing over and comforting the little boy after he is pulled onto the dock.
