Netflix's Too Hot To Handle S3 returns with steamy shower sex, wild snogging sessions and 'one massi

THE third season of Too Hot To Handle is finally here, and we can't wait to tune in.

On January 19, Netflix's raunchiest dating show is returning to our screens, and the newest installment of the series looks like it's going to be full of entertainment.

After announcing its return date earlier this year, Netflix has just released the official trailer for series three, and not only is it jam-packed with 11/10 contestants, they're possibly the horniest yet.

The trailer definitely isn't subtle when showing fans what lies ahead over the 10-part series, with quotes such as "one massive orgy" definitely forcing viewers to listen

A handful of sexy singletons are once again tricked into thinking they're on a completely different TV series, before Lana is revealed, and a ban is placed on all kissing, heavy petting and sexual activity.

This year's lineup consists of Beaux from Kent, UK, Georgia from Brisbane, Australia, Holly from Colorado, USA, Izzy from Manchester, UK and Jaz from Virginia, USA, for the girls.

For the boys, we have Harry from Middlesbrough, UK, Nathan from Cape Town, South Africa, Patrick from Hawaii, USA, Stevan from Los Angeles, USA, and Truth from Texas, USA.

The sexy singletons are raring to go, and as the trailer shows, they are absolutely not holding back this season.

Despite Lana's announcement that the prize fund has been doubled to $200,000 this year, this hasn't held them back.

Scenes in the teaser trailer show steamy sex scenes in the shower, couples getting handsy under the covers, romance between two of the female contestants, and plenty of defiance.

Netflix have said about the upcoming launch: "It's a new year, and it might be snowing outside, but the third season of TOO HOT TO HANDLE is back and steamier than ever.

"It's time to meet a brand new batch of horned-up hellraisers who have just arrived for what they think will be the sexiest vacation of their lives."

They continued: "But with Lana watching, will this wild cast be able to stick to the rules and abstain from any kind of sexual contact (or self-gratification) in order to form meaningful connections, and keep that prize money as high as their sex drives?

"The pressure's on, because this season, the stakes are even greater... with the biggest prize fund on offer, ever."

Too Hot To Handle S3 returns on January 19, 2022 and is available to stream on Netflix
