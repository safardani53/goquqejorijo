How can I get tickets to Lil Yachty's tour?

Who is Lil Yachty?

Miles Park McCollum, also known as Lil Yachty, is an American rapper born on August 23, 1997, in Mableton, Georgia.

In 2015, Lil Yachty gained traction after the release of his single One Night.

The attention he built gained him a spot in XXL 2016 Freshman Class alongside rappers 21 Savage, Lil Uzi Vert, Kodak Black, and Desiigner.

By March 2016, the 25-year-old released his debut mixtape Lil Boat.

Lil Yachty's more notable piece of work was his collaboration with rapper DRAM on the 2016 song Broccoli.

Due to the success of the hit single, the rappers were nominated for a Grammy Award.
