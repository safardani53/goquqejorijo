Kardashians' Best Halloween Costumes | Us Weekly

The Kardashian-Jenner sisters always go all-out when it comes to spooky and sexy Halloween costumes.

From Kim’s sexy cowboy look in 2021 to Kylie’s Cher-inspired getup in 2022, there’s no such thing as a boring costume for the famous family.

In 2023, Kourtney made Us do a double take when she donned the exact Givenchy by Riccardo Tisci gown that Kim rocked at the 2013 Met Gala while pregnant with eldest daughter North. Kourtney, who is expecting her first baby with husband Travis Barker, even rocked the same sleek ponytail and plum lip as Kim did for fashion’s biggest night. Kendall Jenner, meanwhile, decided to put a sexy twist on Jessie from Toy Story in 2022 when she rocked a bandeau top, denim underwear and cow-print chaps.

For Khloé, it’s often about twinning with her kids. In 2023, she and son Tatum matched as a pair of cozy cows. One year prior, Khloé and True dressed up in skin-tight gray kitty costumes.
