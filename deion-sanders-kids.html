Deion Sanders' Kids: Everything You Need to Know [2023 Update]

Deion Sanders is a former American athlete who played baseball and football simultaneously at the professional level. He is the only sportsman who has ever participated in both Super Bowl and World Series.

Deion was born on August 9, 1967, and currently, he is 56 years old. His hometown is Fort Myers, Florida, USA, where he went to North Fort Myers High School as a student.

Deion Sanders’ parents are Mims Sanders and Connie Knight. He also had a stepfather named Willie Knight, who had a positive influence on him.

He played three sports- basketball, football, and baseball when he was at college. In addition, he was also a state honoree in all three sports. However, in 1989 he became a professional in two sports, baseball and football. 

The Atlanta Falcons drafted Sanders in National Football League (NFL) draft. Shortly afterward, he also signed a contract with the New York Yankees to play baseball. As a result, he played simultaneously for both teams.

Moreover, he also became the first-ever sportsman to hit a major league home run and gain a touchdown within a week. In his much more successful professional career, he made an immediate impact.

Sanders intercepted five passes in his rookie season and was a full-time starter in his second season. In addition, he also was named to his first of eight Pro Bowls in his third. 

Later after he retired from his playing career, he founded the ‘Prime Prep Academy Charter School.’ He coached there until 2015, when the school was stopped due to financial insolvency. Presently, he works as an NFL Network analyst.
