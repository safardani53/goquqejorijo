Never Have I Ever Season 3 Is Set To Release On Netflix Soon

Never Have I Ever, Mindy Kaling’s famous comedy series is poised to return for a third season. Season three will premiere on Netflix on August 12, 2022. The plot centers on the life of a young girl called Devi Vishwakumar and the myriad obstacles she experiences after her father’s untimely death. Maitreyi Ramakrishnan plays the protagonist, with Poorna Jagannathan and others playing key parts. Without further ado, keep reading to learn about the release date of Never Have I Ever season 3 on Netflix, as well as the narrative, cast, and other features.

Never Have I Ever Released Time on Netflix, Plot, Trailer, and Additional Information

The third season will be released on Netflix on Friday, August 12, 2022, at 12:00 a.m. PT. The third season of the show consists of 10 episodes. As is customary, Netflix will release all of the season’s episodes on the same day. Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher, co-creators, write the opening and final episodes of the season. Netflix released the official teaser for the third season on July 27, 2022. Devi makes a dramatic entrance to the school with Paxton, startling many people as they see the pair strolling while holding hands. The trailer’s main emphasis is their connection, which is portrayed to be the main topic of conversation at school. Overall, the teaser maintains the show’s upbeat and humorous tone, and fans can anticipate an amusing experience as Devi continues to face everything life throws at her.

Along with the teaser, Netflix has released a description for the third season, which reads:

“In Season 3 of the coming-of-age comedy Never Have I Ever, Indian American adolescent Devi (Maitreyi Ramakrishnan) continues to struggle with the daily stresses of high school and domestic conflict, while also navigating new love relationships.”

Devi’s tumultuous love life was highlighted in the second season, as she was divided between Paxton and Pen. Season two also focused on Devi’s relationship with her closest friends, Fabiola and Eleanor, which went through various ups and downs. With “Daxton” now on the scene, it’ll be fascinating to watch where the tale goes from here. The teaser suggests that things will not be sunny.

More information about Netflix’s Never Have I Ever

Never Have I Ever aired in April 2020 to rave reviews from reviewers and audiences alike, who complimented the show’s engaging plot, comedy, lovely acting, and depiction of South Asians. The series stars Maitreyi Ramakrishnan as Devi Vishwakumar, with Poorna Jagannathan and Darren Barnet, among others, playing important supporting parts.

Mindy Kaling and Lang Fisher co-created the show. To name a few, Kaling has created and starred in numerous successful sitcoms, including The Office, The Mindy Project, and Four Weddings and a Funeral. Never Have I Ever season 3 will be available on Netflix on Friday, August 12, 2022.
