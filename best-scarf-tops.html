9 Best Scarf Tops 2021 | The Sun UK

IT's TIME to channel the trend taking TikTok and Instagram by storm and shop one of the best scarf tops around.

You could just buy a satin scarf and tie it up around you, but the best scarf tops on the highstreet will help you steer clear of any unwanted nip-slips.

Loved by style goddesses Bella Hadid, Kendall Jenner and all the influencers on your feed, scarf tops are the summery must-have of the season.

Shock, horror: it's another noughties trend that's made a 2021 comeback.

They're the perfect combination of bohemian and sexy, a.k.a the ultimate going out top for summer.

If you don't know where to start, fear not because we've found the best scarf tops around. Brunch, anyone?
