Just the Kids This Year!

The annual Kardashian Christmas card used to be something of a holiday tradition, but as Santa’s big day approached, it looked like the first family of reality TV wouldn’t be sharing season’s greetings with their fans for the second year in a row.

Thankfully, just like Old Saint West himself, this year’s card arrived just in time:

As you can see, Kim Kardashian and sisters are nowhere to be found on this simple message spreading tidings of joy.

The younger generation takes the famous siblings’ place, looking stylish (if not particularly festive) in their all-black ensembles.

Obviously, we’ll have to wait a little longer for the debut of Saint West, who likely missed the photo shoot, being mere weeks old and all.

His big sis North is there, of course, alongside Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick’s kids – Mason, Penelope and Reign. 

Which is good, because in the spirit of the holiday, it simply wouldn’t be Christmas without the Lord’s sons.

We’ll show ourselves out.

Anyway, after the glittery fold-out numbers of years past, there are sure to be various theories about why the Kardashian clan chose to go in such a simple direction here in 2015.

We’ve heard reports of Rob Kardashian refusing to participate, or demanding a mammoth sum – refused by his mom – to do so.

Other rumors involved Kylie Jenner throwing a fit after learning that Tyga wouldn’t be allowed on the card, which is just hilarious.

In any case, perhaps Kris decided to avoid all that drama by just focusing on the kids. Or grandkids, as it were, for the family shot-caller.

Hard to blame her. Look at those cuties. Enough said, right? And that’s how she became The Momager Who Saved Christmas.

The End.
