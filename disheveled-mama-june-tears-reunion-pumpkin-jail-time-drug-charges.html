MAMA June Shannon broke down in tears as she was confronted by her family in an emotional reunion.

The 40-year-old reality television star is seen being reunited with daughter Pumpkin, amid her financial woes and drugs arrest in the latest From Not To Hot.

Pumpkin - whose real name is Lauryn - arranged a meeting with the mother-of-four, as they were stuck living in a motel after her mom sold their family home.

She appeared emotional in the sneak peek of the episode from WeTV.

"I want her to know that she's hurt all of us," she stated of her intentions at the rendezvous.

However, she had doubts as to whether it was even worth meeting, after Mama June was arrested on crack charges last year.

"What's the point in sitting down with her if she's on every type of drug that you can name?" she asked, ahead of the meet-up.

The meeting was being moderated by Dr Ish, who was waiting patiently in the room.

"I don't trust her at all," added Pumpkin,

The clip starts with June's daughter Alana "Honey Boo Boo", sending her mom a message.

"We're in a motel room because of you," Alana tells her mom.

Also seen in the episode is her dad Sugar Bear and Jennifer filing for custody of Alana.

It comes as Toddlers & Tiaras star June recently left her daughter Alana in tears on Mama June: Family Crisis after the teen found out she'd sold their Georgia family home.

She reportedly only got a fraction of the home’s value after she put it on the market in August 2019.

Alana's sister and guardian Lauryn was forced to break the devastating news.

She said: “I’ve been trying to find the right time to tell you, mama sold her house and everything in it."

Alana was distraught after learning the home she planned to live in in the future is no longer hers.

“That’s dumb why did she do that? She told me when I got older that was going to be my house! What about my stuff that I left there? We’re never going to get to go back to the house again?

“We had so many great memories in that house and she’s just going to sell it like it was nothing? How can she do this to our house,” the teen cried out.

In August 2019, Mama June sold her home for just $100,000 despite being worth double the amount, according to TMZ.

The low price put her at a loss, as she purchased the home in 2014 $156,000.

June was facing both financial and legal trouble at the time after she and her boyfriend Geno were arrested in March 2019.
