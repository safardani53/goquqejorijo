Kaley Cuoco Nude Photo Leaks; Who Will 4Chan Hacker Hit Next?!

Kaley Cuoco is a viral huge star thanks to her popular Instagram page.

But today fans of the Big Bang Theory actress saw her as they never had before: NAKED!

A scandal that began early Sunday afternoon when Jennifer Lawrence nude photos went viral continues to worsen, with Cuoco as the latest victim of a hacker.

The hacker who’s stolen naked photos from the phones and computers of dozens of starlets. has unveiled an untold number of shots via 4chan and claims he has many more to come.

Lawrence, Victoria Justice, Ariana Grande, Kate Upton, Hope Solo, and Kirsten Dunst are just some of the celebrities who were shocked to be victimized.

Having seen their most personal and intimate images spread across the Internet this weekend, some have spoken out while other stars remain silent.

While Justice has claimed her photos are fake, others have confirmed that their personal pictures were indeed hacked and have vowed legal vengeance.
