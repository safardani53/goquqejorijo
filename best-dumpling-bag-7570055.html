The 21 Best Dumpling Bags of 2024

There’s a mystique that comes with a person’s handbag — whether stars are toting tiny nano purses or filming “what’s in my bag?” YouTube videos, there’s a certain fascination around what makes up someone’s go-to bag. And recently, the fashion side of TikTok has come to a conclusion that Beyond the Vines’ dumpling bag, an adjustable, water-resistant nylon crossbody, may just be the new it-bag that everyone needs in their lives.

Touting thousands of likes on videos showing off just how much you can squeeze into these little dumpling bags, shoppers are raving about the micro dumpling bag for its deceptive capacity and undeniably stylish design. Even celebrities like Jennifer Lawrence are wearing versions of these cinched bags, from the slouchy Simon Miller Lopsy bag and Bottega Veneta Mini Pouch that Kendall Jenner often styles.

“Dumpling bags are trending right now because they are an easy and relaxed style while still being polished,” says Cameron Quittner, celebrity stylist to Niall Horan and Hailey Bieber. “Consumers may be reaching for this casually chic bag over others because it completes a look while still keeping it fresh and on-trend.” The verdict? Everyone’s tired of being so structured — they’re leaning into easy and effortless.

While splashing out on one of these luxury brands would be fun, we’ve found similar styles for a whole lot less than their designer counterparts. Keep reading to discover the best dumpling bag alternatives, from Amazon to Madewell and beyond.

What to Look for Before Buying Dumpling Bags

Construction

As with any bag, a few things you’ll want to keep in mind as you search for that perfect dumpling bag are the design features that make up your pouch. From interior pockets to closures and more, there’s a myriad of features that make each bag unique. Bags like the Uniqlo U Drawstring Shoulder Bag are more functional, offering interior zipper pockets and a fold-over closure (plus a ton of space inside) for all of the things you need, whereas the Banana Republic Pietra Bucket Bag, with its lax drawstring closure and small interior, is a bit more for show (though you can certainly stow your wallet and phone inside). 

Material

There’s no set fabric that these kinds of bags are made from, as this gathered look can be achieved with so many different kinds — from leather to tweed and so many in between. But more flexible fabrics are often the ones that designers will reach for to achieve the most pillowy look. “I like a dumpling bag to be slouchy and any soft material like suede or leather,” says Elisabeth Kassab, the creator of StylistCheck. “To literally look like a dumpling!”

Style 

A “dumpling bag” is the loose term we’re coining these slouchy bags with, because these styles really do come in endless shapes and sizes. From seashell-like clutches (think: Rebecca Minkoff’s Ruched Faux Leather Clutch) to softly-pleated baguette bags, there’s a world of dumpling-esque options out there for you. It’s also important to take into consideration the way you wear your bags — whether you prefer crossbody styles, large carry-all bags, or clutches that can be transformed with a detachable strap. After all, versatility is key.

“I’m seeing lots of circular styles and top handle dumpling bags — very small in size as well,” Kassab says. She calls out this rounded bag from Alva, decorated with a twisted handle and buttery blue suede, as a style she particularly loves. 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a dumpling bag?

    Dumpling bags are any sort of purse that feature a pouch-like structure and a cinched top — forming the shape of a traditional dumpling. These bags come in various sizes and styles, but they all feature a gathered element or type of cinched closure. 

  • Why are dumpling bags trending?

    Dumpling bags are rising in popularity because of a few notable names on social media, such as Beyond the Vines and Uniqlo, but stylists believe that there’s a few other reasons behind the fame. “I think dumpling bags are trending right now because of how effortless and minimal they are,” says Kassab. “No logos, no hardware, so easy to style. They go with anything.”

  • How much should I spend on a dumpling bag?

    While prices and affordability look completely different to every shopper, we think that generally, this is a look that you can attain for (normally) under $200. Our lowest-priced pick, the Ankomina Dumpling Bag, goes for just $17 while a more expensive option, like the Banana Republic Pietra, is on sale for $180. There’s truly no price on style, so whichever style sparks joy in you (and fits your budget) is the one that you should pick when it comes to the price tag.

  • How should I style a dumpling bag?

    “I would keep the rest of the look pretty minimal so that the bag is the star of the show,” Quittner suggests. “A pair of vintage jeans, with a tank, button down, sunglasses, and kitten heels is always my go-to,” she adds, a look that you can take straight from the office to happy hour. “When you identify your uniform, but rotate unique accessories like bags, belts, and sunglasses the possibilities are endless.”
