The One Show fans baffled by Will.i.am's 'ridiculous' outfit - and have burning question about BBC s

FANS of The One Show were left baffled by Will.i.am's outfit as he made an appearance to talk about The Voice.

The American rapper was welcomed on the BBC chat show but viewers poked fun at his choice of clothing as he came to the studio all bundled up.

The judge from recently axed The Voice Kids wore a puffy green coat and matching cap on the programme.

Watchers were left wondering if the BBC had not heated the studio, questioning why else Will would wear such a huge jacket indoors.

One person asked: "Did the BBC with all their cutbacks not put the heating on in the studio for Will.i.Am?"

Another enquired: "They switched the heating off at the Beeb Will?"

A third viewer joked: "He must be sweating buckets!!"

Someone else thought the same, adding: "How roasting hot must @iamwill be in that studio."

The TV show judge previously said he thought the youngsters participating in The Voice Kids are already miles ahead of their adult counterparts.
