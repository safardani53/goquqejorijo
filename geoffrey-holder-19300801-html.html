Geoffrey Lamont Holder is an American actor, dancer, musician and artist from Trinidad. He was the principal dancer of the Metropolitan Ballet before his film career began in 1957 with an appearance in Carib Gold. In 1973, he played the villain of Baron Samedi in the film Bond Live and Let Die. He also did the advertising job as an advertiser for 7 Up.
