[VIDEO] Emmy Rossum Moons Shameless Cast And Crew Hollywood Life

Emmy Rossum Bares Her Rear During ‘Shameless’ Photo Shoot

Emmy Rossum’s character Fiona is absolutely hilarious. During a behind-the-scenes video, fans get to watch her crazy antics first hand. After posing for some more conservative pictures during the season 5 Shameless photo shoot — like running from the cops and playing with fire —  she and fellow co-stars pulled their pants down to their ankles and bared all for one final shot!

Yes, it was all in good fun — and we don’t think anyone on the cast or crew minded. She’s gorgeous!

There was in fact a reason for the stunt, however. Flashing her bare butt was her character’s retaliation to her TV neighborhood changing; that isn’t settling well with her and the rest of the Gallagher family. In the shoot Emmy, Steve Howey and her TV dad, William H. Macy, unleashed their derrières on a restaurant full of unsuspecting customers.

In the video, Emmy reveals that “The Gallaghers hate the fact that the neighborhood is getting better. I think this season’s going to be really good…. It’s the summer which is always fun. The Gallagher’s are outside and rowdy.” Just the way we like them!

Emmy Isn’t A Fan Of Chicago Weather

Emmy was such a good sport, because she Tweeted out just how brisk it was on set — even with pants on!

“Chicago. 20 something degrees and the first snow of the season. Nothing could be better.” She also posted that “It is 12 degrees on set in Chicago. With the wind chill it is 4 degrees. I’m all bundled up to film with Dermot Mulroney for #shameless.”

We’re so excited to see the chaos unfold in season 5 of Shameless!
