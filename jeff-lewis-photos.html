Jeff Lewis Photos Hollywood Life

Jeff Lewis rose to fame on the popular Bravo reality TV series ‘Flipping Out.’ He joined the series at its star in 2007 and hosted alongside a group of ensemble participants. In 2011, Jeff’s partner, Gage Edward, joined the series and brought the couple’s drama to the TV show. The couple was together for roughly 10 years before their split in 2019. Prior to going their separate ways, they welcomed a daughter, Monroe Christine Lewis, in 2016. The pair have, since their separation, been fighting legal battles over who takes on custody of Monroe or how to divvy up responsibilities through co-parenting. Jeff has used his SiriusXM radio show to often air his personal matters, sharing new information with his devoted listeners and fans.
