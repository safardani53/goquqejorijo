Eerie images give chilling insight into creepy abandoned psychiatric hospital where Jimmy Savile sex

THESE chilling images show the decaying remains of a creepy abandoned psychiatric hospital where evil Jimmy Savile preyed on his victims.

High Royds Hospital in Menston, West Yorks, has become a magnet for ghouls wanting to explore the derelict remains.

Spooky images taken inside the abandoned hospital for disturbed youngsters show how it has become a crumbling unit since it closed in 2003.

Paint is peeling from the decaying walls inside the building and the ceiling is rotting away throughout the wards.

A sinister hallway leading up to a metal gate has been plunged into darkness and strong doors that once held patients have been flung open.

The hospital's grand hall has decayed in the 14 years since the hospital was abandoned, and the stage has been covered in graffiti.

A clocktower has been left to rot, but the grounds of the hospital have remained in a good condition.

The chilling photos were taken by an urban explorer known as Exploring Lancashire.

High Royds Hospital first opened in 1888 as the West Riding Pauper Lunatic Asylum where it cared for troubled teens until the late 1990s.

During Operation Yewtree, cops discovered monster Jimmy Savile had abused a patient at the hospital's 100 anniversary celebrations in 1988.

The assault took place during a fancy dress fun, where the sicko also allegedly groped staff.
