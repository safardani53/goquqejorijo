Inside Jennifer Lopez's 51st birthday as she 'cries tears of joy' at personalized cake, flowers and

SINGING superstar Jennifer Lopez confessed to 'tears of joy' as she celebrated her 51st birthday.

The actress shared a video of her home filled with huge bunches of flowers, gifts and a personalized birthday cake.

And as the camera scanned the room, the 60s song 'It's My Party' by Lesley Gore played in the background, with the words: "It's my party and I'll cry if I want to" being sung.

JLo captioned the images and wrote: "Crying tears of joy! Thank you for the best day!"

The huge kitchen was filled with the fancy floral arrangements, which sat next to a dripped icing birthday cake with her name on it and a variety of wrapped presents.

Outside her home, bundles of white and gold helium balloons were also being delivered.

Another post on her Instagram stories showed her enjoying both chocolate and vanilla cupcakes, asking her followers to choose their favourite flavour.

Sharing a montage of well wishes from her fans across the world, the star said: "Thank you so much for all of your beautiful birthday wishes!

"As I watch and read through all of them, I can’t help but think how I spent my last birthday with so many of you last summer celebrating and how this year is so different.

"But what’s the same is I still feel the love from all of you and I hope you feel it too!
