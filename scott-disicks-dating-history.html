Scott Disicks Dating History | Us Weekly

Kourtney Kardashian may be Scott Disick’s most notable ex, but the reality star has been romantically linked to several famous names over the years.

Fans were introduced to Disick in 2007 as Kardashian’s boyfriend on season 1 of Keeping Up With the Kardashians. Over the course of their rocky relationship, the pair welcomed kids Mason, Penelope and Reign in 2009, 2012 and 2014, respectively.

After nearly a decade together, Disick and Kardashian called it quits in July 2015 after Disick was spotted with several different women, including his ex Chloe Bartoli.

Disick later began a long-term relationship with Sofia Richie, who is 15 years his junior, in May 2017. Us Weekly confirmed that September that they were officially an item, but their three-year romance eventually came to an end in August 2020.
