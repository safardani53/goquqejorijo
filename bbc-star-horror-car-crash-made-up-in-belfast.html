A BBC reality star has relived the horrific moment she was thrown through a car windscreen in a terrifying crash.

Made Up in Belfast follows a group of young professionals from Northern Ireland as they navigate the beauty industry.

The BBC Three series goes behind-the-scenes of cosmetic company BPerfect and its boss Brendan McDowell.

But during one episode, the beauty mogul's staff member revealed a horror car crash is the reason she first got into beauty.

Eimhear O'Kane told her work colleague Emma McVeigh that she had scars on her body.

While having a glass of wine Emma asked why, with the retail area manager saying: "I was in a car accident.
