Korina Sanchez (Journalist) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Korina Maria Sanchez-Roxas, known as the professional Korina Sanchez, is a Filipino TV journalist, TV presenter, senior columnist, magazine host, radio host and columnist. She is currently the chief reporter for general news and current affairs of ABS-CBN ABS-CBN Corporation broadcast on TV 2, AM Radio DZMM and ABS-CBN News Channel. She also has a regular column on The Philippine Star titled That Does It, as well as a Tagalog column called K Ka Lang in her sister newspaper, Pilipino Star Righton. She is married to Mar Roxas, former Minister of Interior and Local Government. She switched to the TV5 network after signing with Brightlight Productions.
