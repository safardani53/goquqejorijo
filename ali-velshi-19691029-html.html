Ali Velshi is a Canadian television reporter, senior business and economics correspondent for NBC News since October 2016 and co-host with Stephanie Ruhle of Velshi & Ruhle on MSNBC. Velshi is headquartered in New York. Known for his work on CNN, he served as CNN's chief commercial officer, CNN's Your Money Anchor, and co-host of CNN's International World Business Today weekday business program. In 2013, he joined Al Jazeera America, a channel launched in August of the same year. He ran Ali Velshi on Target until Al Jazeera America ceased operations on April 12, 2016.
