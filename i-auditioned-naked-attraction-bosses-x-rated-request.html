I auditioned for Naked Attraction and was stunned by bosses X-rated request when I stripped off

A TIKTOK user has revealed how she was left stunned by an X-rated request from Naked Attraction bosses.

The hit Channel 4 series sees members of the public strip down to their birthday suits in a bid to find love.

The contestants have to audition for the show, and now one former auditionee has shared their experience with their followers.

TikTok user Kat Baker posted a video to the social media site explaining how she came to meet with Naked Attraction bosses.

She said: "I haven't told you the story about how my little b*** almost got me on TV.

"I was meant to go on that dating show Naked Attraction, you know the one where you get naked and people decide on if they want to date you based on how you look naked.

"Someone messaged me on Facebook and said 'come for an audition, a naked audition' and I did, I went up to London and stripped down in the nude and answered some questions about my dating preferences.

"That video is somewhere, I don't know where but I'd like to see it."

Kat then revealed the production team said they wanted her for the show, but they insisted that she didn't shave off her pubic hair.

She explained how they exclaimed that they needed a variety of women with different preferences regarding their pubic hair, and she was "going to be the girl with the b***'.
