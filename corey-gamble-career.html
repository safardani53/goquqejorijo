What does Corey Gamble do?

COREY Gamble has gained global notoriety as the boyfriend of Kris Jenner.

However, the music mogul has also built a hugely successful career and has worked with some of the best in the business. Here is all you need to know.  

What does Corey Gamble do?

Corey Gamble is a business executive and talent manager who works in the music industry.

As of December 2021, he is associated with SB Projects, an entertainment and marketing company,

Originally from Atlanta, the 41-year-old attended Morehouse College where he studied business marketing.  

He has since built his fortune to a net worth of roughly $3million.

Now residing in Beverly Hills, alongside his partner Kris, he regularly featured on Keeping Up With The Kardashians, which aired its last show on June 10, 2020.

His Instagram account, which can be found @coreygamble, has over one million followers.

Who has Corey Gamble managed?

Corey works for SB projects, which is owned by Scooter Braun, the renowned manager of some of the world’s biggest artists.

Braun’s clients include Ariana Grande, Demi Lovato and Reggaeton sensation J Balvin.

However, Gamble is said to have specifically worked with Justin Bieber – who is set to embark on a world tour in 2022.

The pair are reportedly close friends with Corey referring to the star as his "nephew".

How did Corey Gamble meet Kris Jenner?

Corey met Kris at designer Riccardo Tisci's 40th birthday party in Ibiza and began publicly dating the matriarch of the Kardashian clan in November 2014, after her divorce from Caitlyn Jenner.

The on-off couple has split several times in their nearly seven-year romance.

In March 2017 they split in March 2017 after Kris told Corey "she was getting side-tracked with this relationship and that she needed to put her family before her own needs and wants".

But in 2021, the couple appeared to be going strong after Corey celebrated Kris' birthday on his Instagram page and said: "Happpppppy Birthday my Beautiful Queen!! Love you Deep bab. Cheers To the best mother & more decades of Love and good health with u."

On November 10, 2021, as Corey celebrated his 41st birthday, Kris took her turn to wish Corey a happy birthday on Instagram.

She posted: "Happy birthday to my love @coreygamble!!

"You are the most amazing partner, best friend, confidant, travel aficionado, therapist, problem solver, golf buddy, protector, advisor, and the best, most wonderful man to my kids and my grandkids!!!
