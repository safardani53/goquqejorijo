Nikki Reed and Paris Latsis!

We interrupt your daily Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart relationship updates to bring readers news about the love life of another Twilight star.

Nikki Reed – who plays Rosalie Hale in the vampire-themed franchise – is dating Paris Latsis. Yes, the same Paris Latsis that was once engaged to Paris Hilton!

“They met about six months ago and have been together since,” a source told E! News.

Latsis is a Greek shipping heir that was engaged to Hilton for about six minutes in 2005. But, hey, at least he got to Hilton before the next 47 guys; Reed shouldn’t have to worry about any burning sensations when she pees.

Just how serious is this relationship?

“Both families have met, and really like each other. That’s how serious it’s getting between [Nikki and Paris],” said a friend. “Robert Pattinson is nowhere on the radar. She’s upgraded, in her mind.”

Indeed, Reed was once rumored to be dating Pattinson. Would Paris Latsis really be an upgrade over him?
