Russell Brand and Geri Halliwell: New Couple Alert!

Looks like Russell Brand is looking to Spice up his love life with a new Girl.

That would be Geri Halliwell of Spice Girls fame! See what we did there?!

The 37-year-old British comic is now dating the 40-year-old singer, The Sun (UK) reports, along with pictures of them on a date at Hampton Court Palace in London with Bluebell, Geri’s 6-year-old daughter from a previous relationship.

Russell also posted this Twitpic after the Olympics’ closing ceremonies:

Geri Halliwell recently alluded to a new relationship in an interview with British celeb gossip mag Hello: “I’m not going into details,” she said, giggling.

“Right now there’s nothing serious. I was a late starter on the romantic front. I didn’t start dating until I was in my 30s … I quite like having somebody to cuddle.”

“I like that intimacy, it would be wonderful.”

Earlier this summer, Brand dated Isabella Brewster, the younger sister of Dallas star Jordana Brewster, but the two reportedly broke things off.

Before that, he was married to Katy Perry. Maybe you’ve heard of her. Here’s wishing Russell and Geri the best in their fledgling romance!
