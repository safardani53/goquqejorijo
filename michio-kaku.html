Who is Michio Kaku:

Michio Kaku is a famous Physicist. He was born on January 24, 1947 and his birthplace is San Jose, CA. Michio is also well known as, Physicist who wrote the best-selling books Physics of the Impossible (2008) and Physics of the Future (2011). In 2008 he hosted the three-hour BBC-TV documentary Visions of the Future and in 2009 he began hosting Sci Fi Science: Physics of the Impossible, a 12-episode weekly TV series for the Science Channel.

Michio is originated from United States. He followed in the footsteps of Albert Einstein to become a prominent physicist.
