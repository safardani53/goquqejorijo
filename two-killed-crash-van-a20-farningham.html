Two men, 19 and 20, killed and two others injured in horror crash on A20, near Farningham

TWO young men have died following a head-on horror crash with a van.

Police were scrambled to the A20 in Farningham, Kent, after a red Ford Escort and silver Ford Panel collided.

Tragically, two men aged 19 and 20 who were travelling in the Escort were declared dead at the scene.

Two men in the van were rushed to hospital for treatment but their injuries are not thought to be serious.

Kent Police are now appealing for information following the smash at around 4pm yesterday.

The force said: "The incident happened on the A20 Main Road at around 4pm on Monday 31 July 2023.
