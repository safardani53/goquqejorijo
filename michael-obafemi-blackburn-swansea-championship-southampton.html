Watch Michael Obafemi break down in hysterics again and again in post-match interview after scoring

SWANSEA forward Michael Obafemi couldn't stop laughing during his post-match interview after scoring the winner against Blackburn.

Obafemi was on target after just 16 minutes and led the Swans to a 1-0 victory over Rovers even though Russell Martin's side played with 10 men after Ryan Manning's red card in the 53rd minute.

The striker managed to maintain his composure by netting his first ever home goal during the first half but the same can't be said after full-time.

The Republic of Ireland international and team-mate Jamie Paterson gave an interview to the club's social media team.

But that took a number of takes until the 21-year-old finally got it right as he continuously broke down in hysterics.

Swansea social media manager Sophie Davis repeatedly asked: "Michael, your first goal at the Swansea.com Stadium, a clean sheet, three points.

"It doesn't get much better than that. How does that feel?"

Obafemi tried to answer the straight-forward question but couldn't control his laughter.

Paterson and Davis followed as they also started giggling with the Irish ace's reaction.

The former Southampton ace, though, finally got it together after a number of failed takes and shared his thoughts after a big win over Blackburn.

Obafemi eventually said: “It felt amazing to get that first goal in front of the home fans.

"They have been so welcoming to me and I am so glad I could repay them.

“It just makes me even hungrier to score more goals. It felt just amazing and I need to get that feeling back on a regular basis now and show what I can do.”
