6 Best Mother's Day Gifts For Grandmas 2020 | The Sun UK

MOTHER'S DAY is an extra-special time of year for grandmothers, who not only get love lavished upon them by their kids (hopefully), but by their grandkids as well.

There are lots of fantastic Mother's Day gifts for grandmas to appeal to all kinds of grandmother, from the one who likes a sweet treat to the outdoorsy gardener.

One type of gift certain to delight grandmothers this Mother's Day?

A personalised one: from mugs to cushions, adding a special word, message or name for Granny/Nan/Grandma shows you've put in the extra effort and becomes a gift for grandmas to cherish.

Bonus: they'll especially love a gift personalised with the names of their kids and grandkids to display front and centre in their homes.

Of course, Mother's Day gifts for grandmas that cater to grandma's specific interests are also great.

You can't go wrong with universally loved gifts, from chocolates to flowers, either. Cancer Research UK has a gorgeous online flower shop, with 25 per cent of the proceeds going to fund Cancer Research in the UK.
