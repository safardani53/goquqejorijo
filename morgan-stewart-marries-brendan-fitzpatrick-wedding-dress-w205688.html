Morgan Stewart Marries Brendan Fitzpatrick: Wedding Dress

https://www.instagram.com/p/BFJ6kyNxtkO/

Here comes the bride, all dressed in a white crop top! Morgan Stewart and Brendan Fitzpatrick married on Saturday, May 7, in Santa Susana, California, with the bride in quite the fashion-forward ensemble.

The Touché LA designer, 27, donned a long-sleeve lace crop top, teamed with a sweeping mermaid skirt, both by Badgley Mischka. With her hair in an updo, the Rich Kids of Beverly Hills star added a sheer veil, and carried a bouquet of white flowers in hand.

After the ceremony, the first dance and a bit of celebrating, Stewart made a quick change: She ditched the bridal skirt for a shimmering, extrashort mini.

All the while, her now-husband (and RKOBH costar) modeled a blue Tom Ford tuxedo with a shawl collar, finished with a black bow tie and patent shoes.
