Mads Lewis Is Dating A Fitness Trainer Kevin Mejia

Mads Lewis, a TikTok celebrity, has uploaded a photo with her supposed lover, 28-year-old Kevin Mejia. Fans were taken aback when they saw the photo since the influencer had kept her romance discreet from the internet. Mejia was seen scooping up Lewis by the pool in the photo. TikToker, 19, also tagged the fitness blogger in a boomerang video he shared on Instagram. The influencers are in Arizona celebrating the birthday of a dear friend, Miranda Fauntleroy.

Everything we know about Mads Lewis’ boyfriend

Kevin Mejia has collaborated with well-known personalities. Kevin Mejia is a Colombian who was born and raised in the United States. He is a 28-year-old celebrity fitness trainer who has worked with Justin and Hailey Bieber, Adam Levine of Maroon 5, The Chainsmokers’ Drew Taggart, Kygo, and many more. He is also the head trainer and proprietor of the Dog Pound gym.

Mejia has a significant Instagram following of over 77k followers, where he gives fitness advice and ideas. According to a GQ interview, the influencer’s first work was as a maintenance guy at a nearby Gold’s Gym. When asked what inspired him to start Dog Pound, the trainer stated:

“Everyone should aim to work at the top of the best, and Dogpound was the Mecca for me in this situation. A private gym with boundless enthusiasm and great successful clients/employees on all levels.”

Dog Pound originally opened its doors in New York before expanding to a second facility in Los Angeles. Mejia expressed his excitement about his company’s progress by saying:

“It’s really moving art to have a beautiful facility like the New York location doubled and then magnified in Los Angeles. Seeing a company achieve that, particularly in fitness, is wonderful. And it’s still expanding right now. The coaches are distinctive and motivating, each bringing diverse abilities to the table, resulting in one of the best teams available.”

The relationship between Mads Lewis and Kevin Mejia

Mads Lewis captioned the photo “te amo,” which is the Spanish word for “I love you.” Mejia’s face was obscured in the photo, but she also shared a sweet boomerang video in which she tagged his Instagram account. Fans are interpreting the message as confirmation of their relationship, which first surfaced on the internet in May 2022.

According to a May feature in Glamour Buff, Mejia was Mads Lewis’ gym trainer prior to their growing affair. They were first spotted together on May 16, 2022, when they went to Disneyland with a few other pals. The group took a lot of photos together, but some of them showed Lewis and Mejia as “more than buddies.” Lewis wrapped her hand around Mejia in a gif-style image that featured the whole gang. Another photo showed Mejia photographing Mads Lewis as she posed with one of the park’s mascots. Finally, a picture of them holding hands was shared on social media.

Many followers backed the relationship, but others were put off by the age difference between the two influencers. Lewis is 19 years old, whereas Mejia is 28. Lewis was most recently connected to Jaden Hossler. The couple split up when reports of Jaden cheating on her appeared online. Hossler is presently seeing singer Nessa Barrett, according to speculations.
