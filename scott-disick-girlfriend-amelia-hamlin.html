How old is Amelia Hamlin and what's her net worth?

SCOTT Disick has dated his fair share of young beauties since his split from baby mama Kourtney Kardashian.

The self-proclaimed Lord, 37 is now in a relationship with Amelia Hamlin.

How old is Scott Disick's girlfriend Amelia Hamlin?

Amelia Hamlin was born on June 13, 2001, making her 19-years-old.

The brunette stunner is signed to Women 360 Management New York and has modelled for brands including Hudson Jeans and Too Faced.

Amelia has graced the runway for brands such as The Blonds and Dennis Basso.

She is also no stranger to fame as her mum is Lisa Rinna of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills and her father is actor Harry Hamlin.

When did they go Instagram official?

Scott and Amelia were first spotted together on Halloween 2020.

Though he shares three kids: 11-year-old Mason, 8-year-old Penelope, and 6-year-old Reign with his ex Kourtney, the children reportedly do not spend time with Scott's new fling.

On February 14, 2021 the new couple went Instagram official.

On Valentine's Day Scott, who does not follow Amelia on social media and has never posted a photo of her before, shared two photos of them to his Instagram Stories. 

Appearing to confirm their budding romance, the pair could be seen sitting at a dinner table with a feast of delicious-looking food in front of them. 

What's Amelia Hamlin's net worth?

Amelia has an estimated to fortune of around $1million.

This is thanks to her modelling career and sponsored posts on Instagram.

She also comes from a very wealthy family, as her famous parents have a combined wealth of around $20million.
