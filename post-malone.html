Post Malone Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Post Malone (born Austin Post on July 4, 1995 in Syracuse, New York) is a hip hip and R&B artist. After his breakthrough hit “White Iverson” was released in Feb. 2015, Post signed with Republic Records and has since worked with 50 Cent, Young Thug and others. He will be joining Justin Bieber on the North American leg of his upcoming Purpose World Tour.

Best Known For:
Post Malone is best known for his singles “White Iverson” and “Too Young”.

Personal Life:

Post is self-taught on the saxophone and played in a heavy metal band in high school. At 16, he worked on his first mixtape. He attended Tarrant County College, but did not graduate. After college, he moved to Los Angeles to join the music scene. His signature hairstyle features braids.
