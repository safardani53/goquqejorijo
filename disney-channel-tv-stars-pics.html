Disney Channel TV Stars: Pics Hollywood Life

Hilary Duff, who plays the title role in Disney Channel’s “Lizzie McGuire,” poses in Lizzie’s bedroom on the show’s set in the Hollywood section of Los Angeles. 

Hilary Duff has been one of the many A-list stars that have had their beginnings on the Disney Channel. Others who primarily started their career included Miley Cyrus (Hannah Montana) and Dylan and Cole Sprouse (‘The Suite Life Of Zack And Cody’) just to name a few.

But wait, there are many, many more who launched their careers on The Disney Channel. Zac Efron, Vanessa Hudgens and Ashley Tisdale are still known worldwide for their turns in the ‘High School Musical’ television movies. Selena Gomez, who has since gone on to make huge movies and sold millions of copies, starred on the hit show ‘Wizards of Waverly Place’ beginning in 2007.
