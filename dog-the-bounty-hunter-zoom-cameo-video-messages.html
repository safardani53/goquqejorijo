Dog The Bounty Hunter offering live Zoom call chats to fans for $700

DUANE “Dog” Chapman is charging fans $700 to chat to him over a Zoom video call.

Followers can now head to Cameo.com to book a live meeting with the beloved reality star after he joined the site this year to raise cash.

Dog – who has promoted the service on his social media page - has also been offering personalized video messages to fans for months for $200 a pop.

But now he's gone one step further and vowed to spend 10 minutes of his time with fans who are willing to cough up a bit more cash.

Clicking through to the booking page, the site promises those who sign up: "Have Dog The Bounty Hunter join you for a 10 minute virtual meet and greet!

"Schedule a time for you and up to four friends, family, or colleagues for a live video chat with your favorite celebrity."

The reality TV star first joined the site in March in hopes of connecting with more of his fans.

People have already been loving his short personalized videos to them as some have taken to the comments section to respond.

One person wrote: "Worth every penny! The response time was fast, very personalized message to both my sister & he even said hello to her daughter, Emma, also!

"My sister was literally CRYING tears of joy. I can’t thank you all enough! Dog, you’re awesome! Thank you, thank you, THANK YOU!!"

Another chimed in with: "My parents ABSOLUTELY LOVED their 50th Anniversary video from Dog! He personalized it and took the time to add his anecdotes to make them feel special! Highly recommend Dog! We love you Bruddah!"

One happy fan said: "My mom just about cried. She was so happy! Also, the age joke got us good! We LOVE DOG!!"

Aside from spending time talking to fans and working on his new show Dog Unleashed, Dog has also been working hard planning a wedding with his fiancée Francie Frane.

He recently touched on their relationship in an interview with Dr. Oz.
