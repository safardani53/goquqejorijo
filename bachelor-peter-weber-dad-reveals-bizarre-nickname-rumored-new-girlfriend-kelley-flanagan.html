Bachelor Peter Webers dad reveals bizarre nickname for rumored new girlfriend Kelley Flanagan

BACHELOR star Peter Weber’s dad has revealed his bizarre nickname for Kelley Flanagan.

Peter Weber Sr. fondly referred to his son's rumored new girlfriend Kelley as “Chop Chip” after she wished him a happy birthday.

Peter posted a sweet birthday tribute to his dad in the form of a video on Instagram, which featured himself dancing with Kelly and fellow Bachelor Nation member Dustin Kendrick.

He wrote: “We wish we could be there with you today celebrating, but we’re there in spirit! We love you!”

Kelley went on to wish Peter’s dad a happy birthday, adding some heart emojis next to her comment.

Peter Weber Sr. replied: “Thanks so much for the wishes, Chop Chip. Miss you guys.”

Peter’s mom, Barbara, is just as fond of Kelley.

Last month she described her as “The Most Beautiful, Elegant, Classy, Intelligent Stylish Girl in the world!!!” on Instagram.

While Peter and Kelley haven’t made their relationship official, the former Bachelor has previously admitted he’d be “very happy” if he ended up with his ex.

Speaking on Nick Viall's podcast The Viall Files, Peter confirmed that they are not dating currently, but he could see them doing so in the future.

"Are we dating? No," he began. "Do I love spending time with her? Absolutely. We're not dating.

"Could I see that in the future? Yeah, of course. I'd be extremely lucky and very happy if that happened."

However, don't expect Peter to jump into anything right away as he explained he wants to take things "really, really slow" following his broken engagement to Hannah Ann Sluss and failed relationship with Madison Prewett.

He added: "Of anyone, I'm the last person that needs to rush into any kind of relationship. I just had an engagement that didn't work out.
