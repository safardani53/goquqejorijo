Fake Boobs &amp; Butt Karla Kardashian Hollywood Life

Ellen always goes big for Halloween, but this might be her best costume yet. Fulfilling her dream of being sisters with the Kardashian/Jenner girls, the 57-year-old donned massive fake boobs and filled up with butt pads to resemble the ladies’ enviable curvy figures. She completed the look with an animal print top (with majorly low neckline, of course), and skintight black pants, along with a long, poofy, dark wig. So fabulous!

The talk show host took to Twitter to introduce herself to the world, revealing that she’d be going by the name Karla to go with her costume. She’ll be wearing the look for her Oct. 30 show, where she’ll interview Josh Duhamel, which was taped in advance so we were able to get this fun sneak peek.

Ellen is no stranger to dressing up in busty styles for Halloween. She’s taken on notoriously curvy stars like Sofia Vergara and Nicki Minaj in the past, and the results are always totally epic. We can’t wait to see what the Kardashians think of this one! Some of the funny woman’s other costumes have included: George Clooney and Amal Alamuddin, Snooki’s hair and Jennifer Lopez. Um…can we make this holiday happen EVERY day on The Ellen Show?!
