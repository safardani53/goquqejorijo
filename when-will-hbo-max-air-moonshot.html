When Will HBO Max Air Moonshot? The Release Date, Trailer, Narrative

Moonshot is a highly anticipated sci-fi rom-com that will premiere on HBO Max later this month. Chris Winterbauer directed the film, which was written by Max Taxe. Greg Berlanti, Sarah Schechter, Jill McElroy, and Jenna Sarkin produced it.

‘To Every Boy’ Lana Condor and Cole Sprouse from Riverdale will star in the romantic comedy. Fans have been buzzing about the film’s purportedly fascinating storyline surprise and strong star cast since the teaser was released earlier this month. It’s reasonable to assume that the picture has a lot of hype surrounding it.

When Will The Film Moonshot Be Released?

Moonshot will broadcast on HBO Max on March 31st, 2022 (Thursday) because the film is an HBO Max Original, individuals interested in seeing it will need an HBO Max membership.

Moonshot Trailer

On March 11, 2022, HBO Max published the first official trailer for the film, over a year after its announcement. The trailer’s music is Coldplay’s “My Universe,” which promises futuristic devices and cramped yet intriguing scenes within the vessel.

The teaser suggests that viewers will be in for a wild and entertaining adventure. A plus is an on-screen chemistry between the two protagonists.

Moonshot Narrative

Walt and Sophie are the central characters of Moonshot, a film about two young people with seemingly opposing life ambitions. They end up embarking on a space mission together in order to reunite with their respective love partners. However, throughout their adventurous adventure, the two learn that their true love or soul mate may be right next to them aboard the spacecraft.

Moonshot Cast

Aside from Cole Sprouse and Lana Condor in the starring roles, Mason Gooding from Booksmart and Scream will play Calvin, Sophie’s long-distance lover who lives on Mars.

Captain Tartar, the spaceship’s commander, will be played by comedian Michelle Buteau of The Circle. Ginny is played by Emily Rudd, Calvin Riggins is played by Lukas Gage, and Jan is played by Christine Adams.
