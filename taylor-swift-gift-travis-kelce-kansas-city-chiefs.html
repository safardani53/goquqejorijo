Taylor Swift gifted 4,500 microphone bag from Kansas City Chiefs bosses after romance with Travis

WHAT do you get the woman who has it all?

A crystal encrusted microphone bag, apparently.

The Sun can reveal Taylor Swift was gifted a Swarovski encrusted microphone clutch worth £4,500 by The Hunt family who co-own Kansas City Chiefs.

On Sunday, Taylor was seen cheering on her man, Chiefs ace Travis Kelce, as they beat the Patriots from the VIP section of the stadium.

She was joined by some of the musicians from her sell out Eras Tour as well as singer Alana Haim.

Her dad Scott was seen handing out cake to the neighbouring suites as they celebrated the Shake It Off singer’s 34th birthday.
