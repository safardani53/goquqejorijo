Jennifer Lawrence stuns fans as she strips off and goes totally nude in X-rated new comedy on Netfli

JENNIFER Lawrence has stunned fans by going totally nude in new Netflix comedy No Hard Feelings.

The Oscar winner stripped down to her birthday suit and got into a naked fight on the beach while playing Maddie in the raunchy rom-com.

It tells the story of a desperate delivery driver who agrees to date a wealthy couple's awkward 19-year-old son Percy before he leaves for college.

In one scene, Maddie and Percy go skinny dipping and she's forced to chase pranksters who steal their clothes.

Maddie catches up with them and slaps one teen with her bra before kicking another woman in the crotch.

Viewers get to see the star up close in the buff, and one reacted on X, formerly Twitter: "Jennifer Lawrence is naked in No Hard Feelings? What the f***."

Another wrote: "Jennifer Lawrence is Freggin hilarious in No Hard Feelings. She's completely fearless, gets all kinds of butt naked, and is a foul mouthed truck driver. If I didn't respect her so much I'd probably ask her to marry me."

The actress said she wasn't shy about stripping off for the flick which first premiered in cinemas in June.

She said while promoting the film: "Everyone in my life and my team is doing the right thing and going, ‘Are you sure? Are you sure? Are you sure?’ I didn’t even have a second thought. It was hilarious to me."

And despite the raunchy nature of the movie Jennifer, who gave birth to her son Cy in February 2022, is not worried about her child seeing her movie when he grows up.

She told TalkRadio: "Everything I've learned from other actors is that your kids will never ever want to watch your movies or hear about them."

The former Hunger Games star added: "So I feel safe."

The movie isn't the first time she has gone fully frontal naked though.

She played a former ballerina who is involuntarily drafted into the Russian secret service to act as a seductress in Red Sparrow, but admitted it wasn't as easy.

She said in 2018 that the graphic naked scenes were an "actual nightmare".

She said: "It was really scary to say yes to the [Red Sparrow] movie because I knew the only way to tell the story is if I agreed to really do the scenes and go full Monty.

"It took a while to say yes – and the night before I didn’t sleep at all and I was obviously really nervous because it is the actual nightmare of standing in front of a classroom naked. But then after I finished the scene, I felt empowered."

That year she also spoke about the impact of her nude photo leak.

She admitted there was "a long period when I didn’t want to be seen sexually and I didn’t want anybody to see my flesh".
