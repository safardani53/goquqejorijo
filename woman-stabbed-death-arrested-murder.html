Woman stabbed to death and another in hospital in double knifing as man and woman held for murder

A WOMAN has been stabbed to death and another is hospital after a double knifing - with a man and a woman arrested on suspicion of murder.

Cops were called to Neeld Crescent in Wembley, North London at 9.59am this morning to reports of a stabbing.

Two women were found at the scene with knife injuries.

One of the women, aged 27, was pronounced dead at the scene.

The other woman, aged, 28, was rushed to a North London hospital after paramedics and an air ambulance attended the scene.

A Met Police spokesperson said that the woman's injuries are not life-threatening.

Now a man and a woman have been arrested on suspicion of murder, and remain in a North London police station.

A police spokesperson said enquiries are underway to identify and inform the two women's next of kin.

Crime scenes are still in place and the Met's Specialist Crime Command has been notified.

Anyone who witnessed this incident or has footage are asked to call police on 101 quoting 2380/13JUN.
