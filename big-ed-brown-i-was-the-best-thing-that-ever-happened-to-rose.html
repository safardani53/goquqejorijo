Big Ed Brown: I Was the Best Thing that Ever Happened to Rose!

90 Day Fiance villain Big Ed Brown has had a lot to say about himself and Rosemarie Vega.

But they were able to have a real reconciliation on 90 Day Bares All … albeit virtually.

On 90 Day: The Single Life, Ed’s love life isn’t going as smoothly as he might hope.

But then, whose is?

"What was great about that is I got to be complete with Rose," Ed expressed to People.

"And I got to apologize," he added. "And I was kind of forgiven."

Ed explained: "We both kind of decided ‘let’s move on.’"

"She had always said, ‘This was the worst thing that ever happened to me and you came to the Philippines,’" Ed recalled.

He continued: and you just took advantage of me and used me.’"

That certainly accurately characterizes how Ed treated her on their season.

But Rose has recently found success for herself … and Ed is eager to take credit.

"She’s in a better financial situation, and that’s because of us meeting, than she could ever imagine," Ed bragged.

"She’s very successful with her online business. She’s modeling, she added a second story to her sister’s house," he noted.

"Today, I apologize. You’ve helped me in so many ways," Ed told her on 90 Day Bares All.

"I’m very happy. I’m growing," he acknowledged.

"I still have a lot to learn," Ed expressed.

‘But you admired me and you touched my heart," Ed told Rose, "even though it didn’t work out."

On screen, Rosemarie told Ed that she was "happy to accept" the apology.

She also expressed that she is happy for anyone who might want to one day have children with him, as Ed froze his sperm.

"I don’t really regret it because, first of all, it already happened," Rosemarie expressed.

"I accept everything because that’s the past and it became part of my life," she explained.

"And also, for me to regret it, for what? For what purpose?" Rose asked. "Am I right? Because I moved on already."

"Well, I’m happy that you’re happy and I don’t regret meeting you at all," Ed assured her.

"You changed my life for the better. So thank you," he concluded.

Meanwhile, it seems that lessons that Ed did not learn from Rose are continuing to make life more challenging than it needs to be.

Big Ed decided to ask out a 29-year-old (at least, he thinks that this is her name), even though he is 55.

“I’m glad that I’m wearing a mask to cover up my facial expression,” Liz confessed to the camera when Ed was out of earshot.

“I guess I just never really pictured him asking me out," she added. "We are really good friends.”

Liz then coyly said to the camera that it was too early to see if there is a "spark."

Many fans have said that any woman under 40 who’s dating Ed right now is solely looking for fame.

Even if that’s true, who can fault Liz for going with it after being asked out? As for the "spark," we’ll have to wait until next week to find out.
