Photos Of The Bachelor Nation Star Hollywood Life

Emily Maynard is a member of Bachelor Nation. She was a contestant on season 15 of ‘The Bachelor’ and got engaged to Brad Womack. When her relationship with Brad didn’t work out, Emily eventually became the star of ‘The Bachelorette’ for season 8 of the hit ABC dating series. She picked Jef Holm and broke Arie Luyendyk Jr.’s heart. Things between Emily and Jef fizzled out and they broke up in 2012. Emily married Tyler Johnson in 2014, and the couple has five children together as of 2022.

Emily also has a daughter, Ricki, from her previous relationship with ex-fiance Ricky Hendrick, a former race car driver who died in 2004 in a plane crash. Ricky died before his daughter was born.
