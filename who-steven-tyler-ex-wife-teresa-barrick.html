Who is Steven Tyler's ex-wife, Teresa Barrick?

A WOMAN of many strengths and skills, Teresa Barrick, a former costume and clothing designer for the legendary band Aerosmith, has lived many lives.

The American fashion designer is best known for being the ex-wife of Aerosmith lead singer Steven Tyler.

Who is Teresa Barrick?

Teresa was born on March 26, 1960, in Tulsa, Oklahoma.

She began her career as a fashion and clothing designer, which led her to meet her future husband, Aerosmith's Steven Tyler.

They were introduced by Barrick’s twin sister, Lisa Barrick.

When they met, Tyler was already married to Cyrinda Foxe and shared a daughter with her, Mia Tyler.

He also had Liv Tyler from an earlier relationship with Bebe Buell.

Tyler and Foxe divorced in 1987, and he began dating Teresa.

They dated for six years before they tied the knot in 1988.

Tyler and Teresa were married for 17 years and had two children; a daughter named Chelsea Anna Tallarica and a son named Taj Monroe Tallarico.

What happened between Steven Tyler and Teresa Barrick?

Tyler had dealt with substance abuse issues before their marriage.

According to The List, as the famous rock star stopped his addiction to drugs, rumors of infidelity began.

Tyler’s manager brought the claims of cheating to Barrick's attention.

Shortly after, in 2006, the couple divorced.

What is the former couple up to now?

The couple's daughter, Chelsea, followed in Steven's footsteps and became a musician.

She and her husband, Jon Foster, are the musical duo, Kane Holler.

The couple has two children: a daughter named Isabella Rae Foster and a son named Vincent Frank Foster.

Steven Tyler is currently dating his assistant Aimee Preston.

The couple made their relationship public in 2016.
