Who was David A Arnold and what was his cause of death?

POPULAR comedian David A Arnold was known for his stand-up shows and Netflix specials.

On September 8, 2022, it was announced that he died at the age of 54.

Who was David A Arnold?

Arnold was a popular stand-up comedian, sitcom writer, producer, and actor who was born on March 15, 1968, in Cleveland, Ohio.

Since stepping into the spotlight over 20 years ago he has been seen performing on several stations, including Comedy Central and BET.

He also released two Netflix specials and created the popular Nickelodeon series That Girl Lay Lay.

When he wasn't on stage he could often be found at home spending time with his wife, Julie Harkness.

What was David A Arnold's cause of death?

Arnold's death was first confirmed by his family in a statement.

"It is with great sadness that we confirm the untimely passing of our husband, father, brother and friend, David A. Arnold," the family said, via Deadline.

Later on, the family confirmed that he died from natural causes.

"David passed away peacefully today in his home and doctors have ruled the cause of death due to natural causes. Please keep our family in prayer and respect our privacy at this time, as we are all shocked and devastated by this loss," the statement continued.

Shortly after his death was confirmed, Nickelodeon released their own statement remembering the star.

“We are shocked and incredibly saddened by the sudden passing of our dear friend and creative partner, David A. Arnold,” the network said.

“David was an immense talent and a gifted storyteller with a wide range of fans, from adults through his stand-up, to kids and families through the Nickelodeon show he created and executive produced for us, That Girl Lay Lay. 

"On behalf of everyone at Nickelodeon, we send our thoughts and condolences to his family, his friends and his fans.”

He was survived by his wife and their two children, Anna-Grace and Ashlyn.

What was David A Arnold's net worth?

Due to Arnold's extensive career, he was able to amass an extensive net worth prior to his passing.

While an exact number is unclear, it was reportedly around $4million.
