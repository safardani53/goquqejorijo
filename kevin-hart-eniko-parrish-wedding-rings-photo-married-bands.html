Kevin Hart, 37, and Eniko Parrish, 31, are the cutest couple ever! We are so happy that these two love birds are officially married and their wedding looked out-of-this-world.

Kevin Hart & Eniko Parrish — See Pics Of The Happy Couple

Eniko opted to wear two different gorgeous dresses — her first was a Vera Wang gown with a tight, sheer lace bodice and her second was a sheer lace, long-sleeve gown. Both dresses were equally as gorgeous, but we have to talk about their rings. Kevin posted a photo of their brand new bling and we are freaking out because they’re gorgeous.

[jwplatform ib1U0vu8]

Eniko’s engagement ring is just a diamond band completely covered in diamonds. The band goes perfectly with her insanely huge french-set cushion-shaped halo with a french-set band, engagement ring. She highlighted her gorgeous new bling with such a cool iridescent manicure and we’re obsessed with all of her wedding day glam.

Kevin’s ring on the other hand, is a huge thick band completely encrusted with both big and little diamonds and in the photo they’re literally glistening. Plus, his new wedding band goes perfectly with his diamond embellished black Rolex watch.

Expert celeb jeweler, Shari Fabrikant of Robert Fabrikant Inc. in NYC, told HollywoodLife EXCLUSIVELY.”Her engagement ring appears to be a round diamond set in a cushion shape thick pave diamond halo. The band of the engagement ring is completely set with pave diamonds. Eniko’s wedding band is a platinum pave diamond band to match the side of her engagement ring. Kevin’s band is also a white Gold or platinum pave diamond band. His band appears to have stations in it, similar to Cartier Love ring. Quite a lot of bling on both those hands! If both rings are platinum and diamond and engagement ring has at least a 2.50ct round diamond in it, they would run over $100,000!” Wow, that definitely is quite a lot of bling!
