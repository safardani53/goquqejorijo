Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar slammed for having Josie, 13, wear 'unsafe' bathing suit after she spark

FANS have slammed Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar for having Josie, 13, wear an unsafe bathing suit on their family vacation. 

Over the weekend, Jim Bob and Michelle's daughter Joy-Anna, 25, shared a 23-minute video from the Florida beach getaway.

While the Counting On alum filmed her three-year-old daughter Evelyn on the sand, sister Josie, 13, appeared in the background. 

During her walk, the teen donned a swimming dress with a white undershirt and knee-length skirt. 

Josie pulled her hair back into a messy bun as she stood near the foamy waves. 

THE COMMENTS

In the comments section of an online thread, critics blasted Josie's famous parents for allowing her to wear "a skirt that could slow her down and get in the way."

WEARING A HARNESS

In January, fans became concerned when they spotted Josie wearing a harness fitted around her neck and shoulders during a family meal.

The device appeared to be a figure-8 brace, which is designed to help treat clavicle fractures, broken collarbones, and dislocated clavicles.

Fans shared their worries for Josie on another online thread.

One concerned fan wrote: "One of my students wore something like that when he broke his collarbone.

"I hope that's not the case."

Another fan added: "Could be a flexible back brace to help with borderline/mild scoliosis."

'LITTLE GLITCHES'

Josie was born about 25 weeks into her mom Michelle’s pregnancy - three and a half months premature.

Michelle, 57, suffered from preeclampsia - high blood pressure during pregnancy - and had an emergency C-section to save her and Josie's lives.

Josie is a micro-preemie, which means she is susceptible to long-term health difficulties like cerebral palsy, blindness, and deafness.
