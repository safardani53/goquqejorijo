Scary good! Neve Campbell is an actress who swept onto the scene in the 90s, quickly becoming one of Hollywood’s favorite leading ladies, especially in horror and drama. After getting her start on the Canadian drama ‘Catwalk’ in 1992, she got her big break with the Fox teen drama ‘Party Of Five’ in 1994.

Continuing her ascent to fame, Neve starred as Sidney Prescott in Wes Craven’s 1996 slasher ‘Scream’. She was hailed as an All-star final in the critically acclaimed horror movie, reprising the role in 4 sequels. From there, Neve continued to work hard, appearing in ‘Wild Things’ (1998), ‘The Company’ (2003), and ‘House Of Cards’ (2016-2017). Most recently, she’s appeared in the Canadian drama ‘Castle in the Ground’ (2019) and the musical drama ‘Clouds’ (2020).
