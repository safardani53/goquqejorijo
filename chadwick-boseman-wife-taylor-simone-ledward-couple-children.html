Who is Chadwick Boseman's wife Taylor Simone Ledward?

CHADWICK Boseman passed away at his Los Angeles home following a four-year battle with colon cancer, with wife Taylor Simone by his side.

So who was the beloved Black Panther star's wife and did the couple have any children?

Who is Chadwick Boseman's wife Taylor Simone Ledward?

Chadwick married longtime love Taylor Simone Ledward before he passed away at the age of 43.

Taylor is a singer who graduated with a degree in Music Industry Studies from California State Polytechnic University Pomona in 2014.

She was the lead singer of the school's jazz band at university.

She also once auditioned to be on The X Factor, according to some reports.

The couple maintained a private relationship since they were rumoured to have been engaged back in October 2019.

The last time that the couple was spotted together in public was when they sat in courtside seats at the NBA All-Star Game six months ago.

While accepting the award for Outstanding Actor in a Motion Picture, Chadwick thanked his wife during his acceptance speech, saying: "Simone, you're with me every day. I have to acknowledge you right now. Love you."

She mouthed back the words, "I love you."

Taylor was by his side in his final moments along with close members of his family as he passed away at home.

Speaking to InTouch Weekly in 2018, Taylor's grandmother said the couple were "very happy" and "respect each other".

When did the couple get married?

It’s unclear when the couple were married but are thought to have tied to knot while Chadwick was harbouring a secret health battle.

Chadwick asked Taylor to marry him in October last year after dating for four years, reportedly in Malibu.

The pair are believed to have said their vows in a private ceremony before the actor's cancer advanced.

Chadwick was diagnosed with colon cancer just one year into the relationship.

His death came just 10 months after Chadwick is said to have proposed.

The private pair kept much of their relationship a secret.

As the actor became known for a number of roles - most notably the lead in the Disney action-hero flick Black Panther - the couple were regularly pictured at award ceremonies and red carpet premiers.

Did they have any kids?

The couple did not have any children together, although Taylor's sister reportedly said the pair "would make some beautiful babies."

Chadwick's family confirmed the beloved movie star’s tragic passing on Twitter and revealed he had first been diagnosed with stage 3 colon cancer in 2016 - two years before the release of his Marvel blockbuster.

His passing comes following months of fans worrying for the Marvel star’s health after he was pictured with drastic weight loss.
