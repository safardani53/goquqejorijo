Halloween Cookie Challenge Season 1 Is Set To Be Released On Food Channel

Halloween Cookie Challenge, a new Food Network program, will debut on September 26 at 10 p.m. ET. The program, which was created just for Halloween, aims to promote unusual cookie-baking methods for this frightening time of year. Season 1 of Halloween Cookie Challenge will run for six episodes and will challenge competitors to bake Halloween-themed cookies. By the conclusion of the sixth episode, the participant who has impressed the judging panel will be crowned Halloween Cooking Champion and will receive a large financial award.

More information on Halloween Cookie Challenge.

Every episode of Halloween Cookie Challenge Season 1 will feature five participants. They will compete to see who can make the best-looking and best-tasting Halloween cookies. Every one-hour edition of the program will include challenges designed to make the competition more difficult. In addition to creating cookies that look in a particular manner, entrants will be expected to flavor them. The participant who wins all of the tasks throughout the course of the six episodes will be crowned champion and will receive a $10,000 cash prize. The competition will be conducted in two rounds for the first episode of Halloween Cookie Challenge, dubbed Halloween Costumes and Haunted Houses.
