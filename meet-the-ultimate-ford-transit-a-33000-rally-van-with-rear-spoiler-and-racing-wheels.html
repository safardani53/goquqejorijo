Meet the ultimate Ford Transit - a 33,000 rally van with rear spoiler and racing wheels

WHITE van man just got cool - with this £33,000 rally-inspired Ford Transit.

Fitted with racing wheels and a sporty spoiler, the custom-made van is the perfect ride for any petrolhead delivery driver.

Based on a standard Ford Transit Custom, the MS-RT makeover has been hand-crafted at a South Wales factory.

There's a completely new bumper and grille plus a front diffuser that wouldn't look out of place on a WRC car.

The wheel-arches have been given a steroid hit and there's a big rear spoiler strapped to the back.

It also comes with a British-made twin sports exhaust system, along with unique MS-RT exterior branding and decals.

The 18-inch racing alloys combine with lowered suspension springs to make the van feel sportier on the road.

Under the bonnet is Ford's 2.0-litre diesel engine producing 167bhp.

While outside the van is ready for the track, the inside is kitted out with luxury touches rarely seen in a Transit.

There's handcrafted MS-RT nappa leather and suede seats, a MS-RT sports steering wheel with carbon fibre inlays and MS-RT branded floor mats and dash clocks.

It gets Ford's latest SYNC3 infotainment system with sat nav, parking sensors, reversing camera, heated windscreen, heated seats and upgraded HID headlights.

The rally van, which starts at £32,995, is available as a two-seat panel van, three-seat panel van, five-seat double-cab and six-seat double-cab.

Van drivers can also select a long or short wheelbases with a tailgate or twin back doors.

Ed Davies, MS-RT founding director, said: "The new MS-RT Transit Custom takes an already excellent van and turns it into something really special, which owners are bound to cherish, and which businesses will use to make their brands really stand out.

"We fully expect it to become the hottest property on the van market, and like many of the people who will buy it, we can’t wait to get the job done."
