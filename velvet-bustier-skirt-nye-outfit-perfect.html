I got a velvet bustier &amp; matching skirt from Garage that's the perfect NYE outfit - the top makes me

RING in the New Year with an upgraded sense of style, said one woman.

She showed off the outfit she threw together for her upcoming celebrations that was not only cute but also affordable.

Brianna (@briannafornes_) shared the video with over 18,000 TikTok followers.

The content creator held up a velvet bustier, $45, and skirt, $50, that she was simply obsessed with.

"I cannot get over how cute this set is from Garage and I'm so excited to show you guys," she said.

She changed into the two-piece, showing off the form-fitting corset top and maxi skirt.

"It's the way that Garage ate with this set, look at this, it's so beautiful and fits perfectly," she said.

She revealed she picked up the pieces in a size small but despite that, the outfit wasn't "overly tight."

Best of all, a feature of the top highlighted her figure and gave her a bit of a boost.

"I love that the top kind of goes up, it gives an illusion so it really makes you look super snatched in," she said.
