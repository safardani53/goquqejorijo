Parents racing to get their hands on Aldis 4.99 reusable nappies reviews say are 'better than m

PARENTS are loving Aldi’s £4.99 reusable nappies which they say are friendly for their baby and the environment too. 

Reusable nappies are becoming more common as Brits continue to be more environmentally conscious. 

They don’t always come cheap but are often worth the value as your baby will get lots of use out of each individual one. 

Luckily, Aldi has come to the rescue with a great value option for your little one.

The product in question is the Mamia Reusable Nappy which costs £4.99.

The nappies come in three different designs but can also be bought as a set of three for £14.97.

They’ve been met with rave reviews on Aldi’s website where parents have praised them for being “soft” as well as “quick drying” and “a brilliant price.”

One person wrote: “These nappies and inserts are very soft, slim and light. I think they could be wider to cover the thighs more, but that might be due to my baby's size. 

“The poppers and velcro feel a bit delicate but they're fine as long as you're not too forceful with them.”

A happy customer added: “These nappies are great, quick drying and a brilliant price! They also do not contain nasty chemicals and keep nappy rash at bay. A must if you care about the environment.”
