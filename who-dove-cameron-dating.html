Who is Dove Cameron dating?

FORMER Disney Channel star Dove Cameron is both an actress and singer.

On Saturday, March 4, 2023, she received the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice award for Breakout Artist.

Who is Dove Cameron dating?

As of 2023, Dove Cameron doesn't appear to be publicly dating anybody.

She has a history of longstanding public relationships.

Beginning in 2013, Dove started dating Liv and Maddie co-star, Ryan McCartan.

The two were briefly engaged before calling off their relationship in October 2016.

She was later linked to Kiersey Clemons, although the two never confirmed or denied anything.

She and Descendants co-star, Thomas Doherty, began dating near the end of 2016.

After four years together, the couple split.

"The decision was incredibly difficult, but we still have love for each other, and will remain friends," Dove wrote on Twitter at the time.

When did Dove Cameron come out?

On October 7, 2020, Dove Cameron wrote "me when i date a man / me when i date a woman" attached to a tweet that is no longer available.

One user responded, "this is a confession?" to which Dove added, "girl i been bi."

The same year, she went on social media to address her sexuality, adding that she is "super queer," per Gay Times.

In a 2021 interview with the outlet, Dove opened up about her experience with being queer.

"I went on Instagram Live and said 'Guys, I really needed to explain something to you,'" she said at the time.

"'Maybe I haven't said it, but I'm super queer. This is something I want to represent through my music because it's who I am.'"

Dove added: "I've hinted about my sexuality for years while being afraid to spell it out for everybody."

What is Dove Cameron's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Dove Cameron has an estimated net worth of around $8million.

She's appeared in a number of television shows and movies, including: Barely Lethal (2015), Project Runway (2017), Angry Birds Movie 2 (2019), Schmigadoon! (2021), and Big Nate (2022).

Dove is set to portray Bubbles in the live-action reboot of The Powerpuff Girls.

She's also appeared in a number of on-stage performances, including Mamma Mia! (2017) and Clueless: The Musical (2018-19).
