Labi Siffre is a famous Poet. He was born on June 25, 1945 and his birthplace is England. Labi is also well known as, Poet and songwriter who released eleven studio albums between 1970 and 2006, including The Singer and the Song and the spoken word album Monument.

Labi is originated from England. Michael Ball covered “(Something Inside) So Strong” in 1996 and his version hit #40 in England.
