Jersey Shore's &quot;Snooki&quot; Hooks Up With &quot;The Situation&quot;

On Thursday's Jersey Shore, Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi and Mike "The Situation" Sorrentino clashed when he made fun of her "rolls."

But they seem to be on friendlier terms these days.

On E!'s Chelsea Lately Thursday, Chelsea Handler asked Snooki and The Situation if they've hooked up.

"Yeah, yeah, yeah," he replied.

But it's not a big deal, said Snooki.

"It's not awkward at all," she told Handler. "If I wanna make out with him right now, I can. It's not weird."

The Situation also clarified what "hooking up" means.

"On the show, it's just a kiss," he said. "But sometimes, on the road, it's a little more than that. We save the special stuff for on the road."

Asked if he's gotten luckier with the ladies since the show, he smiled. "I like to think [I was popular with women] before the show," he said. "But after the show, it's been a Situation." The cast is heating up in Hollywood.

During a club outing this week at West Hollywood hot spot Voyeur, Paul "Pauly D" Delvecchio said they partied with Lindsay Lohan and "Lindsay's mom," Dina.

But they were the most star struck when they met Leonardo DiCaprio.
