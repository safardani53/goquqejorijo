Jack Black looks totally unrecognizable as School of Rock star sports thick gray beard on stroll in

JACK Black has appeared completely unrecognizable, donning a thick gray beard in new photos.

Photos captured the actor taking a stroll in Los Angeles, California, on Tuesday.

In them, Jack dressed casually in shorts and a SAG-AFTRA T-shirt, showing his support for the ongoing writers' strike.

He paired the look with tan shoes, high socks, glasses, a wide-brimmed hat, and his long salt-and-pepper beard.

The School of Rock star carried a backpack and seemed to be engaged in an animated phone call as he walked down the street.

DOUBLE TAKE

Fans are still not used to seeing Jack with fuller facial hair, although he's sported the look for some time now.

In April, viewers were taken aback by the Super Mario Bros Movie star's looks when he appeared in a hilarious music video dressed as his character Bowser from the film.

Not only did he transform into the spitting image of his character, but he also had his thick gray beard on display.

Jack again was photographed with his fully-grown facial hair in January 2022 while walking around his neighborhood.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS

Jack's look differed from his usual on-screen appearance, where he typically has a shorter beard and hairstyle.

The TV and film star has appeared in hit movies, including School of Rock and The Holiday, over his 30-year career.

He was nominated for a Golden Globe for his work in the former and earned a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

Jack also has a lengthy resume as a voice actor, starring in favorites such as Kung Fu Panda, Shark Tank, and Ice Age. 

He is also the lead vocalist of the band Tenacious D.

As for his personal life, Jack is a father to two sons, Samuel, 16, and Thomas, 14, with his wife Tanya Haden.
