Jeff &amp; Some Aliens - Comedy Central

Jeff is the world's most average guy but his roommates are anything but ordinary. He shares his small one-bedroom, one-bathroom apartment with Sammy, Jimmy and Ted, three aliens from the planet Azuria. The extraterrestrial trio has been sent to Earth to observe the intricate complexities of humans. Though their intentions may be good, their actions usually just end up wreaking havoc on Jeff's life. The animated series began as a recurring sketch on 2014's "Triptank" before being spun off into its own show.
