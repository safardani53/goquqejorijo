Robert Williams | Career

Basketball for Robert Williams was more like a game to ease his pain away in his initial phase. He commenced playing the games in his high school days and has not stopped yet. 

By the end of his high school, Robert had already established himself as the consensus four-star recruit. He became the number one prospect in Louisiana while ranking 50th overall in ESPN’s Top 100 for the class of 2016.

After entering Texas A&M University, Robert bagged both the SEC Defensive Player of the Year and 2nd Team All-SEC honors.

Initially, Robert Williams showed his NBA Draft interest in 2017 but only entered the following year in 2018. Everyone stated him to be a potential lottery pick for the 2017 NBA draft and 2018 NBA drafts.

However, when he entered the 2018 NBA Draft after losing the 2018 NCAA Men’s Basketball Tournament, Boston Celtics took him in as the 27th overall pick.
