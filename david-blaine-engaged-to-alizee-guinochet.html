David Blaine: Engaged to Alizee Guinochet!

David Blaine has really pulled a rabbit out of his hat this time.

He’s getting married to his girlfriend, French model Alizee Guinochet!

A source close to the couple confirms that the romantic engagement occurred on Blaine’s birthday, April 4: “Alizee’s a great girl. She’s really smart and talented,” the source said. “They are so in love.”

David Blaine, 36, and Alizee Guinochet, 23, were spotted in Central Park over the weekend sporting matching “pre-engagement” rings. Awesome.

Renowned for his flashy brand of street magic and grueling death-defying tests of endurance, Blaine also had a bit of a reputation a ladies’ man.

During the ’90s and ’00s, Blaine was romantically linked with a number of stars, such as Fiona Apple, Bijou Phillips, Daryl Hannah and Madonna.

Congrats to the happy couple!
