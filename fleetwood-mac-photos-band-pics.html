Photos Of The Classic Rock Band Hollywood Life

Fleetwood Mac is one of the most iconic rock bands of all time. It was formed in 1967 by Mick Fleetwood, Peter Green and Jeremy Spencer. John McVie later joined and his and Mick’s names were merged to create the name Fleetwood Mac. Future members of the group included Stevie Nicks and Lindsey Buckingham. They were part of the band’s most iconic album, ‘Rumours.’ It includes the hit songs ‘Go Your Own Way,’ ‘Don’t Stop,’ and ‘Dreams.’ Fleetwood Mac won the Grammy Award for Album of the Year for ‘Rumours’ in 1977. Fleetwood Mac has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
