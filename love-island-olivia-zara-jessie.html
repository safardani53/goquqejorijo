TWO-time Love Island star Adam Collard has revealed the moment he got the “ick” with Zara Lackenby-Brown - as he slammed her “territorial” behaviour. 

The 27-year-old says the model bombshell’s outburst at Olivia put him off - especially when she repeatedly called her “bruv”. 

Zara lost her temper earlier this week when Olivia pulled Tom for a chat.

They're currently caught up in a love triangle - with Zara adamant she'll win.

Adam told The Sun in an exclusive interview: "You're on Love Island - no one is anyone's property. No one should be anyone's property in real life anyway.

"It's down to Tom to say whether he wants to close down Olivia and we've seen he's not done that.

Adam is still single five years on from his first stint on the hit show.
