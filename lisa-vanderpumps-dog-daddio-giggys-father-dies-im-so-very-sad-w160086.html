Lisa Vanderpumps Dog Daddio, Giggys Father, Dies: Im So Very Sad

No, Daddio! Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Lisa Vanderpump is mourning the loss of one of her beloved pups, she revealed via Twitter on Friday, December 18.

“My little Daddio passed away whilst we were traveling back, daddy of Giggy whom we adopted two years ago,” she shared with fans, along with a snapshot of the adorable little pooch in a Letterman jacket. “So very sad.”

The 55-year-old reality star is famously in love with her family of pets, which also includes swans and golden retrievers.

The distraught Vanderpump Rules star has noted to fans that she “can’t even look at” photos of Daddio and his son Giggy together. “It’s been a shock for sure.”

Vanderpump’s daughter Pandora similarly shared a snapshot on Twitter of herself cuddling the little furball.

“My poor baby… I miss you already Daddio,” she captioned the sweet snapshot.
