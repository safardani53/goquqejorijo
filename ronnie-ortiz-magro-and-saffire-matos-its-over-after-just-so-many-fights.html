It's Over! After Just SO Many Fights!

According to various sources, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro may or may not appear on Jersey Shore: Family Vacation Season 6.

His professional fate remains a question mark.

But while we wait to learn to see what the future holds for Ronnie and his job, we now have a pretty clear idea of what the future holds for Ronnie and his alleged soulmate.

Which is to say…

… they have no future.

The Sun reports that Ronnie and Saffire Matos has called off their engagement.

Moreover, Matos has moved out of Ronnie’s home in Los Angeles and returned to the East Coast.

Yes, folks, just about a year after agreeing to marry, the couple has seemingly broken up for good.

“They were fighting like crazy in early June, and called it quits by the middle of the month,” an insider told The Sun on Wednesday, pointing to how neither Ronnie nor Saffire follows the other on social media any longer.

That basically says it all, doesn’t it?

Just five days before this news broke, blogger MTV Reality Teaa [sic] captured a since-deleted TikTok by Matos that hinted at a possible separation.

In the footage, the aspiring entrepreneur sat in a car and wrote: “Broke up with him because our signs didn’t match, I’m a Libra and he’s a lil b—th.”

Oh, well. That also says it all, we guess.

In April of last year, Ronnie was arrested for domestic violence. (Not for the first time, either.)

Although never confirmed, the victim was presumed to be Matos, who refused to testify against her then-lover, prompting the charge to be dropped.

“I take all experiences as lessons,” the MTV personality said after the arrest.

“You learn who really cares about you when your down and out and than you see who doesn’t [sic]! But I guess they call it the weeding process.”

Elsewhere, while Matos now apparently thinks Ronnie is a “b–ch,” she defended him with passion after the 2021 arrest.

“Ronnie and I are fine there is a lot of misleading information floating around out there,” Saffire said back then.

“With anybody who reached out with concern I appreciate it with all my heart, but everything that’s being portrayed out there are not facts. I don’t think it’s fair that people are using false accusations to gain attention.

“Please leave Ron and I alone.

“You don’t know what someone is going through at the end of the day. Don’t believe everything you hear or read online. I’d appreciate privacy please. Thanks for all your support.”

After dating for less than a year, the disgraced reality star got down on one knee and proposed to Saffire in June 2021.

“I love you,” Ronnie captioned a photo via Instagram showing off his fiancée’s diamond ring.

“Thank you for sticking by my side through thick and thin! Til death do us part! #SheSaidYes.”

Ronnie shares a three-year old daughter named Ariana with ex-girlfriend Jen Harley, who he has also been accused of assaulting in the past.

All the way back in November, we first heard there were serious problems between Matos and Ortiz-Magro.

As for whether Ronnie ever comes back to Jersey Shore?

“I’ll be back,” he told TMZ months ago.

“All the fans love me, and I love them too, so I’m gonna give them what they want, and I’ll see them soon.”
