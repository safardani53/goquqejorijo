Susanna Reid stuns as she shows off sleek new hairdo on Good Morning Britain

SUSANNA Reid stunned as she flaunted her new sleek hairdo on today's Good Morning Britain.

The TV star took to the airwaves alongside former politician and Strictly Come Dancing star Ed Balls.

Susanna, 52, was dressed in a pink leopard print body-con dress as she presented the flagship breakfast show.

Usually her glam team styles her tresses into light waves, but this morning her brunette locks were completely straight.

Her hair was styled into a distinctive middle parting as she interviewed the news-makers of the morning.

This included her grilling of Defence Secretary Grant Shapps who was live from the Conservative Party Conference in Manchester.

"The team has thrown a little lookalike picture together, and you're clearly rocking it with those straighteners."
