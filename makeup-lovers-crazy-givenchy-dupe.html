Makeup lovers go wild for 'luxurious' 4.99 designer lip gloss dupe which is just like Givenchy ori

MAKEUP Revolution has launched a £4.99 Givenchy lip balm dupe just in time for the holidays.

The low-cost brand has released it's Ceramide Swirl Lip Gloss, and they're obsessed.

They say it's just like the Rose Perfecto Givenchy lip balm - which retails at £30.

Makeup Revolution is known for its dupes, offering alternatives to brands including Charlotte Tilbury.

They offer £4 Urban Decay eyeshadow dupes, £5 dupes for the Stila Magnificent Metals Foils and countless other high-end beauty alternatives.

Their newest dupe has turned heads.

The Ceramide Swirl Lip Gloss is priced at £4.99 and available in five different shades.

Many shoppers were thrilled to see the gloss after it was shared on Instagram. One said: "It's the Givenchy gloss but a cheaper dupe."

A second commented: "Givenchy is this you?"

A third Revolution fan said: "It reminds me of strawberries and candy canes!"

The product page said: "Indulge your lips with this high-shine gloss, The Revolution Lip Swirl Ceramide Gloss leaves your lips looking glossy & hydrated without the sticky feeling!"

"Each shade gives you a soft, natural tint of colour while being packed full of hydrating ceramides to lock in your lips moisture, maintaining that glazed lip look."

The beauty brand claims the gloss features a "luxe" marbled swirl effect to help shoppers feel "bougie on a budget".

The dupe lip gloss comes in a range of shades, from a "deep purple tint" to a clear gloss.

The Sun discovered other incredible dupes for brands like Charlotte Tilbury, including the e.l.f Halo Glow Liquid Filter.

The e.l.f. liquid foundation is claimed to be a perfect dupe for Charlotte Tilbury's £36 Hollywood Flawless Filter.

If you're looking for a dupe of The Airbrush Flawless Setting Spray from Charlotte Tilbury, the NYX Professional Makeup Plumping Setting Spray is long lasting, hydrating and effective and only costs £8.

The formula is vegan and a few spritzes will keep your makeup in place and refresh your skin.
